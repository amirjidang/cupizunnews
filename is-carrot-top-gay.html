A video on his Instagram featuring a Pride armband only added fuel to the gossip.

It’s worth noting that Carrot Top’s appearance has evolved over the years due to surgeries and facelifts, leading to a more feminine look that fueled assumptions about his sexual identity.

In a 2015 interview with Lasvegasmagazine, Carrot Top addressed the ongoing rumors, acknowledging that some comments about his appearance and sexuality stung a bit, but he brushed them off with his trademark self-deprecating humor, saying:

“People say, ‘Carrot Top is gay’ or ‘Carrot Top got a facelift.’ Would I look better with a facelift? Or ‘Carrot Top does drugs.’ It’s so absurd and people say to me, ‘That hurts your feelings, right?’ Some of it does. But I’m a big fan of self-deprecating humor; that’s my whole thing.”

Despite the persistent speculations, Carrot Top has exclusively dated women in the past, dispelling any doubts about his straight orientation.

So, while the rumors may persist, his dating history speaks louder than gossip when it comes to understanding his actual sexual orientation.
