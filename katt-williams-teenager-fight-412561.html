Teen Involved In Fight With Katt Williams Shares His Side Of The Story

Publish date: 2024-08-07

A video surfaced yesterday (March 23) that had everyone watching in bewilderment at what they just witnessed. Comedian Katt Williams apparently engaged in a scuffle with a 17-year-old boy named Luke Walsh, following a dispute during a soccer match.

The full visual shows Williams and the teen exchanging words and swiftly Williams was placed in a headlock that’s served as a screenshot around social media. Now, Walsh is sharing his side of the confusing story.

In an interview with the Daily Mail, Walsh said everything was going fine during their friendly soccer game, “but stuff happens in sports.”

He then continued, “He took me over to a secluded area and asked me to hit him and I was like, ‘I’m not just going to hit you. You want to square up?’ And then he sucker-punched me so I did what I did.”

Following the break-up of the altercation, Walsh attempted to let bygones be bygones. “I tried to shake his hand and he was like, ‘back off me little boy.’ I don’t know if he was sour but it is what it is,” he said.

An investigation has been launched, but no charges have been made thus far.
