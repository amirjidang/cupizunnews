A Peek Inside Pete Davidson's $1.3 Million Staten Island Condo

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Situated near the coast of New York borough, the luxurious condominium comes with unhindered views of the cityscape. In February 2022, the former Saturday Night Live comedian announced he would be shifting base to Brooklyn permanently. One of the reasons for this significant lifestyle change on the NBCLX show was the long commute over the Staten Island bridge towards the main island. The bachelor pad is termed the most exclusive luxury property on Staten Island. The King of Staten Island actor purchased the modern condo for $1.2 million in December 2020.

Earlier, Pete lived in the basement of his mom’s residence, and he moved into the modern condo to accommodate his then-girlfriend Kim Kardashian while they briefly dated.

A Prime Property On Staten Island

The luxury high-rise condo has sweeping views of the New York City skyline. According to People, the 1,592 square feet apartment includes two state-of-the-art bedrooms and two and a half bathrooms. The Hawaiian-style powder room is the main attractive feature of this open floor-spaced condo. It is situated in the historic neighborhood of Georgetown on the north of Staten Island. The real estate in this area is famous for single professionals and entrepreneurs. There are numerous art galleries, nightclubs, and trendy restaurants for entertainment. The daily commute to Manhattan is feasible since it is near the Staten Island ferry.

The stylish bachelor pad is in the opulent Accolades building, and the vicinity offers unrestricted views of New York Bay. The building complex provides a 24x7 guarded community, and the nine-story building accommodates 103 units. Other facilities include a yoga studio, common playground, game room, barbeque area, resident lounge, and waterfront typical garden. According to Apartment Therapy, the Accolades building also features a clubhouse, business, and tennis club. Sitting in a prime location, the affluent condo is a perfect living space for singles.

The Gorgeous Open Spaces Plan With Plenty Of Natural Light

An open floorplan that merges the living room with the dining and kitchen area welcomes guests through the main door. The hardwood flooring complements the neutral and earthy tones of the whitewashed walls and large glass windows that throw in ample natural light. High-rise ceilings impose a spacious vibe on every corner of the contemporary apartment. According to the New York Post, A private sitting area bridges the living space between the powder room and the bedrooms.

The master suite is done in white and comes with a large walk-in closet. The main bathroom features stone-lined tiles, a marble vanity area, a Jacuzzi tub, an individual shower unit, and ornate tiles. The powder room is done up with a trendy Hawaiian green wallpaper, adding a whimsical charm to the high-rise apartment. The sweeping New York Bay views through the living room windows are magical for party nights. A colossal blue lounge sofa adorns the living area with a modern entertainment system for cozy movie and game nights. The second bedroom has a set of bunk beds for guests and family, and the walls are done in earthy tones for a tranquil ambiance. The luxurious condominium is designed for functionality and a rich experience on the waterfront.

Ideal For Romantic Nights And Entertainment

According to Homes And Gardens, the primary selling point of this luxury condo is the private terrace that opens up through the main suite. Situated on the seventh floor, it gives panoramic views of the Manhattan area. Ideal for outdoor entertaining and romantic summer nights. The space is decorated with a large lounge sofa and dining table. The subway-tiled kitchen, too, comes with equal hosting space, perfect as a breakfast nook with luxe countertops and a mini island for meal preps. The immaculately white cabinetry allows ample pantry space for food. The unique feature is the marble waterfall countertops that weave panache into the contemporary décor.

The space is airy and can comfortably seat four people for a modern-day meal. It also has the latest kitchenware range to dish out gourmet meals. A peninsula snack bar adds a fun element to the open space. In-ceiling speakers have been installed in the living area for a home theater effect for movie nights. A snooker table divides the main living room from the kitchen. Pete entertained his then-girlfriend Kim Kardashian at his condo last year. Recently he enjoyed a game night with his current girlfriend, Emily Ratajkowski, at his luxurious condo.

Pete Davidson has listed his luxe condo on Staten Island for $1.3 million. Amy Arnone and Marissa Polo from the Jason Mitchell Group handle the property. They have stated that this prime waterfront property has been an ideal living spot for the Suicide Squad actor. He had an enviable lifestyle at the luxurious apartment, which he will be switching soon with the trendy vibe of Brooklyn.
