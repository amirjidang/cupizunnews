Extreme II: Pornograffitti, released in 1990, propelled the band to stardom. The album reached double platinum status and featured their iconic hits like “More Than Words” and “Hole Hearted”. These songs showcased Extreme’s ability to deliver heartfelt ballads with soul-stirring melodies.

III Sides to Every Story

In 1992, Extreme released their follow-up album, III Sides to Every Story. Songs like “Rest in Peace” and “Stop the World” showcased Extreme’s dynamic range and artistic growth.
