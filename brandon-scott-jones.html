Parents, Wife, & Early Life

Brandon was born in Greensboro, North Carolina. He spent most of his childhood in the neighbouring community of McLeansville with his parents, Kimberly Scott Jones and Reid Jones. He belongs to a working-class family where his father was a corporate officer while his mother was a housewife.

Interestingly, when Jones was a teen, he was an active athlete. He used to play tennis and was a football player at Northeast Guilford High School. After completing high school, Jones travelled across the country Coast and moved to Los Angeles to propel his career and education.

Career

A prominent actor in the Isn’t It Romantic series; Jones started his career in early 2009. During that time, he initiated taking acting professionally. Moreover, Brandon got his career breakthrough when he worked in the 2010 Abercrombie & Fitch holiday camp with famous photographer Bruce Weber.

Afterwards, he got his second big project in 2011, where he featured in German electronic music as the lead vocalist on the single Wants to Be Known. That same year, he got a role in CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, playing the part of the role of the son of D.B. Charlie Russell.
