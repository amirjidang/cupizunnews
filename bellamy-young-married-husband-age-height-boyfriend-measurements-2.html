Age, early life and rise to fame

Bellamy Young was born on February 19, 1971 in Asheville, North Carolina. She was given up for adoption at birth and spent six weeks in foster care before being adopted. Her adoptive mother’s name is Jane Young while her adoptive father’s name is unknown. There is also no indication that she had other siblings. Her parents named her Amy Maria Young and she grew up with the first name Amy.

Young was artistically inclined as a child. She took tap dance and ballet classes and performed whenever she had the opportunity, whether at church or school. The actress was not only artistic, but also very intelligent. She completed her high school education at Asheville School in 1987 before entering the prestigious Yale University. While at Yale, she studied English and drama, graduating in 1991. After graduating from college, Young seriously pursued an acting career. Between 1991 and 2000 she appeared on a few Broadway and Off-Broadway shows with minor appearances on a few TV shows.

Her first professional stage appearance was in the 1994 musical merry we roll on . Other stage productions include Faust (1995) and Life (1997). The latter was her first Broadway show. Bellamy Young moved to LA in 2000 to further her career. At the time, her name was still Amy Young. However, when she registered with the Screen Actors Guild, she found that another actor had already taken the name. She therefore chose Bellamy. Bellamy pays homage to her stepfather, Bill, who took care of her after the death of her first adoptive father.

Since breaking into Hollywood in 2000, BellamyYoung has accumulated an impressive credit consisting of numerous television shows and films. Young’s artistic talents are not limited to acting. She is also a talented singer; The actress released her first album on iTunes in May 2015.
