Halle Berry and Van Hunt's Relationship Timeline

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Halle Berry found something special with Van Hunt and has enjoyed showing off her relationship to her fans.

Berry was previously married three times: to David Justice from 1993 to 1997, to Eric Benét from 2001 to 2005 and to Olivier Martinez from 2013 to 2016. She shares son Maceo (born in 2013) with Martinez and daughter Nahla (born in 2008) with ex Gabriel Aubry.

Berry confirmed her relationship with Hunt in September 2020, shortly before a source exclusively told Us Weekly that Hunt was “a wonderful, caring” partner to the actress. While the pair had only been dating for a few months by then, the insider noted that Berry already saw the musician as The One.

Berry and Hunt made their red carpet debut one year later at the 2021 Academy Awards.
