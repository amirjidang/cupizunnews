Lil Waynes High Performance (Uh?) Ear Buds

Publish date: 2024-08-11

There’s a pretty good chance Lil Wayne has no idea he’s got a pair of ear buds coming out soon. After all, he’s got bigger things on his mind right now. But apparently, Weezy, Shakira and a few other celebrities are going to appear on the packaging for different pairs of ear buds coming out through Live Nation sometime later this year. It doesn’t look like Wayne had anything to do with the creation or design of the ear buds, which are going to be compatible with iPods, iPhones and other MP3 players. But they’re supposed to be priced under $20. Hey, Wayne’s got plenty of mouths to feed these days (pause?), so we can’t even hate on him for getting his money. But, um, there’s a pretty good chance you won’t see these laying on any desks up at the VIBE office anytime soon. Just sayin’… —Chris Yuscavage
