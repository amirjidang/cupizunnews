Gloria Velez is Aaron Hall Baby Mamma – Are They Married?

Gloria Velez is Aaron Hall’s baby mama however, the couple is not married even as of today.

The two birthed their son back in the 90s after being together for some time.

At that time, Velez was an aspiring name in the hip-hop industry as a model.

It was when she met Aaron Hall who was already an established star.

The two got together and had a child when Velez was just in her teens, meanwhile, Hall was in his 30s.

The difference in their age and the quick happening of pregnancy has quite created a stir in the media.

However, the two decided to grow apart and Velez became a single mother to the child, Aaron Hall IV.

Meet Gloria Velez Son Aaron Hall IV

Gloria Velez is a single mother to her son Aaron Hall IV from her relationship with Aaron Hall.

The reason behind the breakup between Aaron and Gloria was the infidelities and abusive nature of Hall, per MTV.

Per the report, Gloria’s public outrage and her words in her songs, videos, and interviews are all revelations of her relationship with Hall.

When she gave birth to her son, she was just 17 years old.

Sadly, she faced abuse and cheating from Hall thus, she opted to raise her son alone.

Hall’s drug problems were also one of the reasons behind their separation.

Since then, Gloria became a solo parent and did not let Hall communicate with their son, Aaron Hall IV.

Aaron Hall IV has grown up to be an artist just like their son, per his Instagram.

Where Is Gloria Velez Today?

Gloria Velez’s current whereabouts are not on display.
