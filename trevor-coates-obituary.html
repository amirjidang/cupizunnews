Trevor Coates Obituary: A Life Cut Short in a Tragic Car Accident

Publish date: 2024-08-28

The sudden and tragic death of Trevor Coates has left his family and friends reeling. Know all about Trevor Coates obituary details. 

The 23-year-old from Brompton Regis was a well-known face in the community, particularly in the Wiveliscombe Rugby Club where he was an active member. 

His passing on Saturday, December 5th, 2020 has left a deep impact on those who knew him. 

An incident that took place on the B3227 near Blackerton Cross in East Anstey resulted in fatal injuries for Trevor, who was a passenger in a Volkswagen Bora that crashed into a tree. The police confirmed his death soon after.

Trevor Coates: A Beloved Son, Friend, and Rugby Player

Trevor Coates was born in Brompton Regis and had lived there all his life. He was a beloved son and friend, with a wide network of people who cared for him deeply. 

His passion for rugby began at a young age, and he soon became an integral part of the Minehead Barbarians team. 

A talented player, he quickly made a name for himself and was known for his tenacity, spirit, and determination on the field. 

He also enjoyed supporting his friends and teammates and was always quick with a word of encouragement or a pat on the back. 

He was deeply respected and admired by those who knew him.

The Collision that Claimed Trevor’s Life

On the evening of December 5th, 2020, Trevor was in a car with several other individuals traveling down the B3227. 

The vehicle they were traveling in, a Volkswagen Bora, lost control on a bend and collided with a tree. 

The impact of the crash was devastating, and Trevor suffered fatal injuries. The other occupants of the car were also injured, but their injuries were not life-threatening. 

Emergency services responded quickly to the scene, and Trevor was transported immediately to a local hospital where he was declared deceased.

Reactions of Trevor’s Family and Friends

The news of Trevor’s passing has left his family and friends in shock. They are currently coming to terms with their loss and are trying to cope in the best way they can. 

Members of the Wiveliscombe Rugby Club and the Minehead Barbarians team have expressed their condolences and paid tribute to Trevor on social media. 

Graham Symes, a member of the West Somerset club, wrote, “A wonderful young man, our condolences and our thoughts to Trevor’s family, friends, and all at Wiveliscombe RFC from Minehead Barbarians RFC.” 

Others have shared memories of Trevor, recalling his infectious smile and his love for the game.

Investigation into the Collision Continues

The Devon and Cornwall Police Department is conducting an investigation into the collision that claimed Trevor’s life. 

They are urging anyone who witnessed the accident to come forward with any information they may have. 

In particular, they are looking for dashcam footage that may have captured the events leading up to the crash. 

A spokesperson for the police department stated, “Any witnesses to the collision who have not already spoken to the police are asked to contact us on 101 quoting log number 633 of 05/12/2020, particularly if they have any dashcam footage. 

Any witnesses who have dashcam footage are especially encouraged to get in touch.”
