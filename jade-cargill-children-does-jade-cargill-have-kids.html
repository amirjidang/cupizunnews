Jade Cargill Children: Does Jade Cargill have Kids?

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Jade Cargill is an American professional wrestler. Jade and her partner Brandon Phillips have a daughter together.

Their daughter named Bailey Quinn was born on April 18, 2017.

Brandon Phillips and Jade Cargill established the Texas Smoke, a new professional softball team based in Austin, in 2023.

The Texas Smoke join the Women’s Professional Fastpitch (WPF) league, which also comprises clubs from Oklahoma City, Alabama, and Texas.

Tori Tyson is the Texas Smoke’s head coach, and Eric Mays is the team’s general manager.

Jade Cargill is currently signed to WWE.  She is most known for her tenure in All Elite Wrestling (AEW)

Cargill made her professional wrestling debut with AEW on the November 11, 2020. During her time at AEW, she was a one-time and inaugural AEW TBS Champion from 2020 to 2023; her reign of 508 days is the longest for any AEW championship.
