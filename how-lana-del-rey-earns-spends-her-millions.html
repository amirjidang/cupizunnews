Elizabeth Woolridge Grant was raised in upstate New York before she moved to Connecticut to pursue high school. While living with her uncle and aunt in Long Island, she learned how to play the guitar and began her songwriting process. While she majored in philosophy, she simultaneously released EPs until 2008. After signing with an independent record label, Grant took the stage name Lana Del Rey to release music. Her self-made videos for Blue Jeans and Video Games quickly earned her recognition, landing her a joint record label deal with Interscope. With the release of her debut album, Born To Die, Rey developed a melancholic genre of music, writing and singing heartbreaking songs that resonated with people.

Lana Del Rey has become one of the most famous artists in the music industry through her original songs and collaborations with notable artists. The singer has amassed a $30 million net worth that she loves to spend on her real estate and helping people everywhere. Let’s look at how Lana Del Rey earns and spends her millions.

Earns: Music Career

While Lana Del Rey had earned considerable notoriety for her EPs, it wasn’t until her debut album, Born To Die, that she became a household name in 2012. The singer has recently released seven studio albums and collected 10.2 million in sales. According to W Magazine, she became the third female artist in 2018 to have an album on the Billboard 200 chart for 300 weeks with Born To Die. Her famous songs include Summertime Sadness, Young And Beautiful, Video Games, Lust For Life, and many more.

Spends: Real Estate

The singer is known for her modest life, and the most significant purchase she made was purchasing a luxury mansion in Hollywood Hills. Located on the outskirts of Studio City, Lana Del Rey knocked down two adjoining properties to create a single-family home. It is valued at $5.4 million and features six bedrooms, seven bathrooms, and stunning canyon views. The house was initially built in 1935 before going through several renovations.

Earns: Model And Fashion Designer

The launch of her music career also made Rey a bookable artist, as many brands approached her with lucrative offers. She earned a modeling job for H&M in 2012 for its fall campaign while she performed a cover of Blue Velvet in a retro-themed setup. Additionally, Emma Hill, the creative director of the fashion house Mulberry approached her to design an iconic bag in 2012, as noted by Vogue. Her haunting music created imagery for the design of the white bag.

Spends: Car Collection

Lana Del Rey's car collection ranges from newer models to vintage automobiles. Her notable cars include the Jaguar XK and Jaguar XJ, which she often drives around LA while running errands. She also owns a black Ferrari California and a Jaguar F-Type. Her most photographed car is a champagne 1981 Mercedes-Benz 380SL, a vintage model she purchased in 2012.

Earns: Brand Endorsements And Literature

Not only has Lana Del Rey modeled and inspired fashion, but she has also become the face of notable brands that feature her songs. As mentioned by HypeBeast, to introduce the F-Type, Jaguar released a commercial with the singer who composed a new song, Burning Desire, for the campaign. As a talented songwriter, she also released a poetry book Violet Bent Backwards Over the Grass, in 2018 that became a New York Times Bestseller.

Spends: Charity

Helping people has always been important in Lana Del Rey’s career, as she does not hesitate to use her platform to raise awareness and support communities. In 2020, she made a generous donation to the Dig Deep Foundation $350,000 to ensure clean water is accessible to every citizen of America. She has also supported two American orchestras that were financially struggling during the COVID-19 pandemic. She donated an undisclosed amount and performed Summertime Sadness to raise money.

Earns: Tour Earnings

There has always been an excitement to hear Lana Del Rey’s melancholic vocals live. The singer has embarked on six tours over the years, mostly limited to North America. Her Endless Summer Tour grossed $6 million, while The Norman Fucking Rockwell! Tour made $2 million before it was canceled due to the pandemic. Her highest-grossing tour was LA to the Moon Tour, which made $22 million in 2018-19.

Spends: Clothing

Known for her love of nostalgic Hollywood and vintage glamor, the singer is often spotted wearing retro clothing with eye-catching jewelry. Her signature looks include dark make-up with old Hollywood style. Over the years, she has given many stunning fashion moments. Rey arrived at the 2020 Grammys in a $500 Aidan Mattox silver gown she purchased at a mall, as stated by Harper’s Bazaar. The sequined dress reflected her whimsical outfit choices and impulsive purchases.

Lana Del Rey has not only released music through her albums but also written music for movies and television shows. Her haunting voice has consistently elevated the score for any project to resonate with the audience. The artist released her latest album in 2021 and was last seen collaborating on the track Snow On The Beach from Taylor Swift’s hit album Midnights in October 2022.
