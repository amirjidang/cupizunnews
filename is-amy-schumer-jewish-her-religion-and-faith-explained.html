Is Amy Schumer Jewish? Her Religion And Faith Explained

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Amy Schumer is Jewish as her father belonged to a Jewish family from Ukraine. 

Amy Schumer is a stand-up comedian and actress of America who was named one of Time magazine’s 100 most influential people in 2015.

Amy is the co-producer and co-writer of the Comedy Central sketch comedy series Inside Amy Schumer. For the series, she won Peabody Award and was nominated for five Primetime Emmy Awards, winning the Outstanding Variety Sketch Series.

Moreover, Amy has been praised for addressing various social issues through comedy

Is Amy Schumer Jewish?

Yes, Amy Schumer is indeed Jewish. She is also half Protestant as well.

She was born to a Jewish dad Gordon David Schumer and a Protestant mother and Sandra Jane Schumer.

She opened her eyes in Lennox Hill Hospital on the Upper East Side of New York City on 1 June 1981.

Amy revealed that being raised as a Jew, she had to deal with antisemitism as a child. She was teased by the name ‘Amy Jew-mer’ which only shaped her humor.

Though she was born into wealth and privilege from her father’s highly successful imported baby furniture business, Lewis of London, she also had to face prejudice against her race.

After her father declared bankruptcy for his failed business, things only got worse.

Even while she was studying at Towson University in Baltimore, Maryland, she faced a lot of difficulty surrounding her ethnicity.

Amy Schumer Religion And Faith Explained

Amy Schumer was raised in the Jewish religion and faith. She even made comedy jokes regarding her religion Judaism.

In her bat mitzvah, she made her first stage debut comedy but it did not go well as her voice cracked as she chanted from the Torah.

As Amy’s voice crackled, the congregation burst out in laughter, and her confidence rose.

Her Jewish essence is also rooted in Jewish food. She once said, ‘I like that I’m Jewish and I enjoy the grossest Jewish food like whitefish salad and gefilte fish.’

Amy Schumer Parents Background And Race

Amy Schumer’s parents are Gordon David Schumer and Sandra Jane Schumer whose background and race are Jewish and Protestant.

Her father, Gordon was born to a Jewish family from Ukraine. He is a second cousin of U.S. Senator and Senate Majority Leader Chuck Schumer.

Her mother, Sandra is from a Protestant family. Her mother’s family belongs to New England roots. Further, Sandra converted to Judaism before her marriage.

Her mother’s ancestry included Puritans in the 17th-century English colony of Massachusetts.

As a guest on a show called Finding Your Roots in 2017, she got to learn that in 1704, three children from her ancestor Thomas Tarbell’s family were captured at Groton, Massachusetts, in a French-Abenaki raid.

They were taken to Montreal and the girl child was rescued by a French-Canadian family. She then joined a French Catholic convent.

The two boys were each adopted by Mohawk families at Kahnawake.
