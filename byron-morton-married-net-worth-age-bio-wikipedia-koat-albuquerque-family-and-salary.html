Byron Morton KOAT Action 7 News

Morton keeps you in the know morning and night on the weekend editions of Action 7 News. He stated“I guess you could call me a weather geek. Weather is what makes me tick and it affects everyone every day.” He works with Joe Diaz and Eric Green who are also meteorologists at the station.

Before joining the Action 7 News team in December 2001, Morton was chief meteorologist at WMSN-TV in Madison, Wisc., and fill-in meteorologist at its sister station, WKOW-TV.

Prior to this, Morton held on-air positions at WOI-TV in Des Moines and WAOW-TV in Wausau, Wisc., and served as weather producer for Good Morning America, The LA Times, and Televisa Mexico. Morton also interned at KFOR-TV in Oklahoma City.

Morton is the second forecaster in the state (after Chief Meteorologist Joe Diaz) to earn the prestigious designation of “Certified Broadcast Meteorologist” from the American Meteorological Society (AMS), and he holds a seal of approval from the National Weather Association (NWA).
