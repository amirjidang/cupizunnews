Fans Are Deeply Saddened By Demi Moore's Plastic Surgery

Publish date: 2024-08-20

When Demi Moore hit the runway to open for Fendi, fans were met by a far different version of her that made them do a double-take.

Celebrities are known to frequently pursue plastic surgery options, so it's not surprising to see a famous face altered in some way. However, it seems that Demi Moore's facial cosmetic procedure was taken way too far. Fans were not only shocked, but were also deeply saddened to see what the star has done to alter her natural beauty.

Demi Moore, on the other hand, took to Instagram to reveal how significant this very moment was for her as she crossed the stage for Fendi, seemingly unaware of the fact that her fans had an entirely different perspective.

Demi Moore Shocks Fans

When Demi Moore shared her big Fendi moment with fans on Instagram, it's unlikely that she expected to receive a response like this one. She began by telling fans how important and meaningful this experience was to her. Clearly more than a job or a moment of status, Demi Moore had a strong emotional connection to opening the runway for Fendi at the prestigious Paris Fashion Week event. She posted a very chic video on Instagram, alongside the caption; "Fulfilling a teenage dream... Thank you @mrkimjones for having me open the @Fendi SS21 show and congratulations on a beautiful and magical debut!"

Fans that were initially drawn to her commentary and clicked to see more, were suddenly met with an unfamiliar face, and there didn't seem to be a single comment suggesting Demi's aesthetic change was for the better. This "teenage dream" of hers didn't imply she had to try looking like a teenager.
