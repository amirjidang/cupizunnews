Dana Bash Salary: How much money does Dana Bash make?

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Dana Ruth Bash is a CNN journalist, news anchor, Inside Politics host, and co-anchor of State of the Union.

As a chief political correspondent at CNN, Dana Bash is under contract that pays an annual salary of $3 million.

Dana Bash worked as a producer for CNN after graduating from college. She was in charge of producing weekend shows such as “Late Edition,” “Evans & Novak,” and “Inside Politics.”

Dana Bash later began producing programming about the United States Senate, and as a result of her efforts, she was named CNN’s chief congressional correspondent.

Among her other career highlights have been interviews with Senator Rob Portman, Representative Gabrielle Giffords, and Senators Rand Paul and Ted Cruz.

For CNN, Bash has covered numerous presidential primaries and debates, as well as midterm elections. She moderated the 2019 Democratic presidential primary debate and anchored special coverage of Joe Biden’s victory in the 2020 election.

Dana Bash began co-hosting CNN’s Sunday morning programme “State of the Union” with fellow anchor Jake Tapper in 2021. Bash has received numerous awards for her journalistic work. She was a member of the team that won the Joan S. Barone Award for excellence in congressional reporting in 2012.
