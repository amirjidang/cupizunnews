Who are Leon Draisaiti Parents? Explore His Family!

Leon Tim Draisaiti was born on 7th October 1995 in Cologne, Germany, to Peter and Sandra Draisaitl. He is a mix of half-Czechoslovakian and half-German by ethnicity. He has held German citizenship since he turned 18.

Draisaiti’s father, Peter, is a Czech-born German retired pro ice hockey player. He played as a center for West Germany and Central Germany ice hockey teams. Following his retirement, he served as a head coach of the Kolner Haie hockey club.

Similarly, Leon’s mom, Sandra, is a perfect homemaker and caretaker of his entire family of four members. She has been serving as a part-time back-office manager of the marketing and advertising agency WANT Agentur since August 2009.

Leon’s sister, Kim Draisaitl divides her time in Amsterdam and Koln, Nordrhein-Westfalen, Deutschland. She is a film and television producer by profession. She produced a TV movie documentary, GDR – The Disposed of Republic, in 2019.
