Alterraun Verner is an American football player with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. His net worth is estimated to be around $12 million.

Alterraun Verner started his football career as the wide receiver for Mayfair High School. He earned second-team All-CIF Southern Section honors. Also, he was selected to play during the Shrine All-Star Game.

In college, he joined the UCLA Bruins and he was selected first-team Freshman All-America by the College Football News and Scripps. Verner is one of the 12 semifinalists of the Jim Thorpe Award in 2009.

He was chosen by the Tennessee Titans during the NFL Draft in 2010.  He was selected as AP Second Team All-Pro and AFC interceptions co-leader in 2013.

Alterraun Ennis Verner was born on December 13, 1988, in Orange, California.
