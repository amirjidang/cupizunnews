Marriage with Rafael Nadal

Maria Francisca Perello’s relationship with Rafael Nadal started long before they tied the knot. The couple began dating in 2005 when they were teenagers, and they managed to keep their relationship private for three years before it became public knowledge.

In 2019, after 14 years together, they exchanged vows in a beautiful ceremony held in their hometown of Mallorca. Perello wore an elegant long-sleeved gown designed by Rosa Clara.

Perello has been a constant source of support for Rafael Nadal throughout his career. She is often seen in the stands, cheering him on during his matches, while maintaining a low profile and letting her husband shine in the spotlight.
