Taylor Swift Allegedly Drinking Too Much At Coachella With Travis Kelce Has Divided Fans

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's time at Coachella sparked speculation about Swift possibly being drunk, with a lip reader's analysis.
  • Fans had mixed reactions about Swift's alleged drinking, with some praising the couple's happiness and others expressing concern.
  • Despite the rumors, Kelce and Swift enjoyed performances together at Coachella.

If Taylor Swift isn't going to headline the epic music festival Coachella, it makes sense that she would want to have a great weekend there with her NFL tight end boyfriend Travis Kelce. While it remains to be seen if Swift will write any songs about Kelce like she might have written tunes about exes like Tom Hiddleston, the beloved couple definitely like to have public date nights. Considering the football player's high net worth and, of course, Forbes naming Swift a billionaire, it's no wonder Kelce and Swift can attend any event they please.

There was one moment at Coachella 2024 that got people talking, and it naturally included Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce. Page Six reported that a lip reader thought Taylor Swift said she was drunk. In this article, we'll take a look at whether fans believe that Swift was drunk at Coachella, along with a closer look at the couple's fun time at Coachella.

Do Fans Think Taylor Swift Drank Too Much While Partying At Coachella With Travis Kelce?

According to Page Six, Jackie Gonzalez, who competed on Squid Game: The Challenge, read Taylor Swift's lips and shared what she believes the singer at Coachella on Instagram and TikTok. Gonzalez is deaf and lip reads regularly.

According to Gonzalez, Swift sang during Ice Spice's performance of her "Midnights" song "Karma" and then Swift allegedly told Kelce she drank too much:

“That was the best thing that’s happened tonight. Sorry, I’m drunk."

Several Taylor Swift fans believe she had too much alcohol at Coachella and that what Jackie Gonzalez wrote was right. Page Six quoted fans who were glad to read what Gonzalez said. One fan said, “so cool to know what she is saying. You narrated that so good especially the cheering part."

Inside Taylor Swift And Travis Kelce's Time At Coachella

Besides watching Ice Spice's "Karma" performance, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce watched Bleachers perform at Coachella. Considering that Jack Antonoff writes songs with Swift, that makes sense. It seems that besides wanting to have a great weekend with her boyfriend, Swift wanted to support her musician friends.

According to a source who spoke to Life & Style Magazine the couple watched Jamie Kennedy DJ at the Neon Carnival at Coachella. The event was put on by PATRÓN EL ALTO tequila. The source said, “Travis walked into the area first and was joined by Taylor, Ice Spice and a few others, in addition to a crew of security guards. Taylor and Travis cozied up as they enjoyed cocktails and bopped their heads to James’ set, which was a blend of house and pop music.”

Fans also praised Swift's fashion choices at Coachella. According to E! News, she wore a skirt that cost only $35, which people found relatable.
