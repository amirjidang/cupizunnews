Abby Lee Miller Says Taylor Swift Dances Like A Pigeon, Travis Kelce Has Better Moves

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Highlights

  • Dance Moms star Abby Lee Miller criticizes Taylor Swift's dance skills, implying that her boyfriend, Travis Kelce, is a better dancer.
  • Abby believes it may be too late for Taylor to improve her dancing, but acknowledges that Taylor is likely more focused on her successful singing career.
  • Abby's comments about Taylor and Travis' relationship join a chorus of concerns voiced by others, but Taylor has not responded to the controversy.

Taylor Swift is used to her fair share of haters, but there’s one person she may have not seen coming for her, Dance Moms star Abby Lee Miller, who recently called out the pop singer’s dancing skills.

During a Leave it on the Floor podcast appearance, Abby made scathing comments about Taylor’s dance skills, an irony considering the pop star has been flaunting her moves on her record-breaking Eras tour this year, which has generated over a billion dollars in profits this far.

Abby Says It’s Too Late For Taylor To Become A Better Dancer

“Taylor Swift is pigeon-toed,” Abby, who appeared on the TLC show from 2011 to 2017, said. “And her boyfriend is a much better dancer. Let’s leave it at that. He’s a great dancer.”

Abby was referring to Taylor’s current boyfriend, Travis Kelce, known for his career as an NFL player with the Chiefs. Given that Travis isn’t known for dancing at all, Abby’s comments were a clear diss towards Taylor.

Though Abby said she thinks it’s “too late” for Taylor to improve her dancing, she doesn’t believe the musician is concerned about it, given how successful her singing career has been with the dance moves she currently has.

“I don’t think she’s worried about it,” Abby went on. “I don’t think that’s her top priority right now. However, I think she does probably think that she should have danced as a kid. You know, they had her slumped over that guitar playing. And I loved her music then. That was the Taylor Swift that I fell in love with.”

While Abby didn’t comment on Taylor’s relationship with Travis any further in the podcast interview, she did reply to a TikTok video discussing her comments about the duo. Abby used it as an opportunity to further praise Travis’ dance skills.

Everyone can benefit from a class with me,” she commented. “I’ve seen clips of @Taylor Swift’s boyfriend, @Travis Kelce dancing it up both on the field and off. I think he’s a natural! Most football players like to show off! He’s a showman with swagger at heart!”

Many People Have Expressed Concern Over Taylor And Travis

Abby may be the latest celebrity to comment on Taylor and Travis’ high-profile relationship, but he’s certainly not the first. Apparently, even those close to Taylor have voice their concerns about the romance, which appears to be moving quickly. Taylor and Travis are even facing relentless engagement rumors, despite only going public with their status in the summer.

Last month, a report claimed that Selena Gomez voiced concerns to Taylor over Travis’ intentions, which came after his ex-girlfriend Maya Benberry issued a public warning to Taylor Swift and exposed Travis for cheating.

While Selena didn’t respond to the reports, a similar one accused Gigi Hadid of having reservations about Travis. But the supermodel went on to deny the report, saying she was supportive of Taylor’s romance. For now, Taylor hasn’t commented on any of the controversy over her current relationship.
