It has just been revealed that A$AP Rocky is having his first child with the legendary business mogul and R&B icon, Rihanna. The 33 year old rapper has seen the highest heights of fame through his wildly successful career, and has attained an impressive net worth of $10 million along the way. His girlfriend, and soon-to-be mother of his first child is worth an astounding $1.7 billion. As the world watches in wonder while this supercouple proceeds to grow their family and solidify their bond,  all eyes are on A$AP Rocky, as fans attempt to discover more about the man that has stolen Rihanna's heart. Fans were stunned to learn that his is a true rags to riches story.The artist has risen from a life of misfortune and crime, and has managed to leave his days of homelessness behind, in exchange for a luxurious lifestyle of envy.

A Childhood Filled With Tragedy

Rakim Mayers was born in Harlem, New York City on October 3rd, 1988. His mother is an African-American, and  his father was born in Barbados. He currently leads a life of abundance that is filled with privilege and great fortune, but that hasn't always been the case for the young artist. He started rapping at the tender age of just 9 years old, but what he didn't realize at the time, was that his success would be a long road, and the first part of his journey would be a painful one.

When Rocky was just 12 years old, his father was incarcerated for selling drugs. The family was deeply affected, and just a year later, when Rocky was just 13, his brother Ricky was murdered by a rival drug dealer.

Homelessness And Crime

A$AP Rocky was overwhelmed with grief and loss, and he was watching his family fall apart. Without many options left, A$AP Rocky also went on to sell drugs on the local streets as a means to make ends meet for his family, and during this phase of his life, he was wandering between homeless shelters with his mother and his sister.

Without his father and brother by his side, he became a street hustler and did what it took to keep his family fed as they tried to find someplace to sleep. In 2004 he was arrested for pedaling and served two weeks in jail. It was there that he met Cassanova, who would soon go on to become a prominent rapper.

The Beginnings Of His Career

A$AP Rocky had always been inspired by music, and meeting Cassanova while in lock-up proved to be a pivotal turning point for his career. He soon became part of the Harlem-based group known as A$AP Mob, and it was at this time that he adopted the 'A$AP' moniker that he continues to embrace today.

He saw the release of his first single, Purple Swag, in July of 2011, and he instantly became a huge rap sensation in his local New York community. He continued to focus on his music and just a month later, in August of 2011, his single Peso was leaked online. This was immediately followed by the release of his mixtape Live. Love. A$AP in 2013, which received critical acclaim. It also rose to the top of the Billboard 200 charts. He was gaining traction quickly, and was becoming something of a local celebrity in his home town.

There was just one more thing to do - get a record deal. Lucky for A$AP Rocky, he didn't have to wait long for that deal to be sealed. He signed with RCA Records and Polo Grounds Music for $3 million, and simultaneously started his A$AP Worldwide label.

A$AP Rocky was officially a star, and his bank account was starting to surpass his street cred. He has gone on to enjoy the success of several top-charting albums and is now growing his fame and fortune exponentially through various income streams, inclusive of brand collaborations, hot new music, and his own, dedicated business ventures.

Large Scale Collaborations & Projects

A$AP is now a formidable player in the rap world and has gained worldwide fame and critical acclaim. He boasts a variety of top charting songs and has received more accolades than can be listed.

Throughout his successful career, he has collaborated with the likes of Gucci Mane, Tyler the Creator, 21 Savage, Frank Ocean, Drake, Kanye West, Future, Rod Stewart, and Kid Cudi, among many other notable names.  He has also appeared in a number of movies, and continues to produce records under his alias name, Lord Flacko.

A$AP Rocky also joined forces with Rihanna musically on several occasions, and through an endorsement deal in which he lent his celebrity status to promote her Fenty line for men.

In addition to his musical career, A$AP Rocky also debuted his own clothing line in 2013, in collaboration with prominent designer Raf Simons. He penned a huge contract for worldwide representation with William Morris Endeavor, and also released a special line with Guess which was called GUE$$, and saw Rocky's personal influence on the legendary brand.

Lavish Lifestyle

These days, A$AP Rocky is living life in the lap of luxury. He has released an endless library of music and combined with his other projects. He has more than enough money to sustain his lifestyle, and spoils himself by purchasing all the items his heart desires, without any financial hesitation.

Among the many high-priced items that he owns, is A$AP Rocky's $3 million mansion Harlem. The house boasts cutting edge, modern architectural designs, and sits on 4300 square feet of meticulously landscaped land. The mega mansion has 5 enormous bedrooms, 6 bathrooms, a spa, and an Italia-inspired kitchen made of glass, steel, and marble. He has a huge pool and a private patio which includes a fireplace.

His home is filled with his immense sneaker collection, containing a number of Air Jordan and Rick Owens shoes. It's also filled with other expensive items that A$AP is obsessed with, including a number of custom grills, one of which is embossed with his nickname and valued at just over $10,000.

His jewelry box is overflowing with a number of thick chains and pendants, and one in particular is covered in diamonds and has set him back $300,000. A$AP Rocky is also obsessed with watches, and owns a number of unique time pieces, inclusive of some of the most expensive Rolex watches that money can buy.
