Who is JT Gasso?

Publish date: 2024-08-05

JT Gasso is the assistant coach of softball at Oklahoma University. His primary responsibilities include working with the Sooners’ outfielders and the offence.

During Gasso’s tenure, the Sooners have won five Big 12 regular-season titles and three Big 12 Tournament championships as well as five Women’s College World Series appearances and three national titles.

The 2021 club rewrote the record books under Gasso’s offensive coaching. The offence hit an NCAA record 161 home runs while also setting NCAA records for team batting average (.405), slugging (.778), on-base percentage (.490), runs scored (638) and total bases (638). Gasso contributed to senior Jocelyn Alo’s record-breaking season, as she blasted a program-best 34 home runs her route to being named 2021 USA Softball Collegiate Player of the Year.

Gasso graduated from Eastern Oklahoma State College with an associate’s degree in exercise science, East Central University with a bachelor’s degree in exercise science, and the University of Oklahoma with a master’s degree in administrative leadership.
