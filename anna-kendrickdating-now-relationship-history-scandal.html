Anna Kendrick Is Extremely Secretive About Her Relationships But Here's What We Do Know About Her Lo

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Anna Kendrick is a popular actress and is well-known for her comedic roles. She lives her life int the limelight and has given countless interviews to publications over the years, but when it comes to her private life, this is a topic she has mostly avoided speaking about. She has an interesting (although not particularly long) dating history with some of her ex-boyfriends also being involved in the entertainment industry. But, not all of these past relationships have ended well, and not all of them were healthy.

Kendrick has given fans a glimpse into her life with a poignant interview about a toxic relationship. Although she still didn't name names, fans likely have a theory as to who this was...

Who Has Anna Kendrick Dated?

Unlike some Hollywood stars, Anna Kendrick does not have a very long list of past relationships. Her past romances include director Edgar Wright, whom she started dating in 2009 before the couple broke up four years later.

Although unconfirmed, Us Weekly also lists actor Jake Gyllenhaal as one of her previous relationships in 2011, and later Ben Richardson from 2014 to 2020.

In 2022, she was reportedly in a relationship with Bill Hader (who appears to have been her last romance, at least that we know of). "They were both single at the same time," a source Us Weekly.

"They're really into each other and make each other laugh — a lot. They're keeping their relationship private, but friends close to them know." The relationship was short-lived, ending in the middle of the same year. Not much is known about the coupling because both stars are incredibly private.

"Anna has been dating Bill quietly for over a year," a source told People Magazine in January 2022. "They met years ago. She's hosted Saturday Night Live, and they've done a movie together, but they got together well after the movie. They are both very private people, and with the pandemic it was easy to keep it quiet. They're both hysterical, so they must keep each other laughing all of the time. She's really, really happy."

Bill Hader told The Hollywood Reporter in 2022 that he does not talk about his relationships because he is a father and wants to give his daughters privacy. "They just want me to be their dad," he said. "They just want me to sit and watch Encanto over and over and over again. So that's what I do."

What Has Anna Kendrick Said About Her Relationships?

Anna Kendrick does not often give interviews about her romantic life, but she has made some comments through the years that give us a glimpse into her personal life.

In a 2017 interview with Elle, she spoke about how she had dumped a former boyfriend because he did not respect her boundaries. "I was dating a guy. He tickled me playfully, and I said, 'I know that's cute and that people do it, but I really don't like being tickled. It really makes me feel trapped and panicked. I know it's silly and funny for most people, but I really hate it, so could you please not?' He thought that it was really dumb that I had a problem with being tickled and did it anyway," she explained.

"I broke up with him. And I knew that in the retelling of that story, I would be some crazy girl," she continued. "You never want to be labeled 'the crazy girl'.... That he would tell his friends, 'Oh, she broke up with me, because I tickled her. What a psycho.' I just had to go, 'No, I broke up with you because I told you something was important to me, and you didn't respect that.' I knew that he wouldn't necessarily frame it that way in his mind, and I just had to be okay with that. Which sucks, because you want to be understood. But that's the person I am…"

Although Kendrick seems to have a good idea of what she wants and doesn't want, this has not always happened as she thought.

"The wrong kind of guy to fall in love with is the guy who will let go of the steering wheel as a joke," she said in a 2015 interview with Fashion magazine (via Us Weekly). "A guy who finds it amusing to make you uncomfortable — which is more common than you'd think — is someone you want to avoid." Although comments she made years later suggested she was with someone who you would want to avoid.

This was revealed in her interview on the Armchair Expert podcast, and it really got her fans' attention because she revealed a lot about being in a toxic relationship.

Who Was The Toxic Ex-Boyfriend Anna Kendrick Spoke About?

Anna Kendrick is pretty secretive about her relationship history, but she has spoken out about one ex-boyfriend (she chose not to name names) and how toxic their time together was.

"I was with someone — this was somebody I lived with, for all intents and purposes my husband. We had embryos together, this was my person," she said in the January 2023 interview (via Vanity Fair). "And then about six years in, about somewhere around there, I remember telling my brother, when things had first kind of gone down, 'I'm living with a stranger. Like, I don't know what's happening.'"

She wanted to make things work for a while, and they tried couple's therapy. "It wasn't just the, 'Oh, I'm losing a relationship.' It was that I believed that if we broke up or, you know, if he left basically, it was a confirmation that it's because I'm impossible, I'm lucky that he's even tolerating my bullshit," she explained.

"There was an inherent thing of me being so rejectable that this person who loved me very deeply for six years, it suddenly occurred to him how awful I was or something. The shame that lingers much longer."

We cannot be certain who this person was, and it would be unfair to speculate, but perhaps her fans have some theories about who he is?
