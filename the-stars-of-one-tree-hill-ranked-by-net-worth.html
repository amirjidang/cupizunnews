American drama television series One Tree Hill was one of the most anticipated shows during its run from 2003 to 2012. The series even premiered with about 2.5 million viewers which grew to 3.3 million in its second week. The season of the show averaged about 3.5 million viewers while the highest rated season two averaged about 4.3 million viewers weekly. The teen series created by Mark Schwahn has gone on to continue their success and popularity for nine seasons. The show has produced new set of stars in the forefront.

Jana Kramer

Music singer and actress Jana Kramer’s current net worth is estimated to be $3 million. The actress was born on December 2, 1983 in Rochester Hills, Michigan. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, she went on to continue her singing career while starring in different shows such as Entourage, Friday Night Lights and 90210. She also released her single Why Ya Wanna which even landed on the top 5 of the country music charts.

Austin Nichols

American actor Austin Nichols’ current net worth is estimated to be $4 million. The actor was born on April 24, 1980 in Ann Arbor, Michigan. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, he went on to star on different movies such as The Day After Tomorrow and Wimbledon. Although One Tree Hill is not is first ever acting role, it was the show that made him popular.

Hilarie Burton

American actress, businesswoman, author and producer Hilarie Burton’s current net worth is estimated to be $5 million. The actress was born on July 1, 1982 in Sterling, Virginia. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, she went on to appear in different series such as Lethal Weapon, White Collar, Grey’s Anatomy and Hostages and Extant. Burton was one of the star of the show which made her the top paid stars on the show as well as she was the female lead. She left the show right after season six with Chad Michael Murray.

Robert Buckley

American actor Robert Buckley’s current net worth is estimated to be $5 million. The actor was born on May 1981 in West Covina, California. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, he had recurring roles on different series like American Heiress and Lipstick Jungle. Buckley was not part of One Tree Hill on its full run; he only joined the casts during the season seven until the last season as Chad Michael Murray left the show.

Shantel VanSanten

American model and actress Shantel VanSanten’s current net worth is estimated to be $5 million. The actor was born on July 25, 1985. in Luverne, Minnesota. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, she continued on to star in different projects such as Gang Related, The Messengers, Beauty and the Beast and The Flash. Just like Robert Buckley, Shantel was not part of the show on its full run, they were introduced after the exit of Chad Michael Murray. Her character on the show, Quinn James, was paired with Buckley’s Clay.

James Lafferty

American actor, director, and producer James Lafferty’s current net worth is estimated to be $5 million. The actor was born on July 25, 1985 in Hemet, California. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, he went on the star on different projects such as Underground, The Haunting of Hill House and Crisis. Although he made different projects after the show, his most notable role to the public was when he played Nathan Scott in One Tree Hill.

Bethany Joy Lenz

American actress, director, producer, and musician Bethany Joy Lenz’s current net worth is estimated to be $6 million. The actress was born on April 2, 1981 in Hollywood, Florida. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, she continued to pursue her career as a musician, producer and director while still doing some minor roles. When One Tree Hill was finished she then appeared on CSI and Dexter.

Sophia Bush

American actress, activist, director, and producer Sophia Bush’s current net worth is estimated to be $9 million. The actress was born on July 8, 1982 in Pasadena, California. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, she has starred in different TV shows such as The Hitcher, John Tucker Must Die and The Narrows. She was one of the lead of the show who married another lead of the show Chad Michael Murray. However their marriage did not last long as they divorced after one year of marriage.

Chad Michael Murray

American actor Chad Michael Murray current net worth is estimated to be $12 million. The actor was born on August 24, 1981 in Buffalo, New York. After the successful run of the show One Tree Hill, he went on to do different projects such as House of Wax, Freaky Friday and A Cinderella Story. Murray was one of the biggest actor the show has produced even though he only stayed until season six and then left the show with Hilarie Burton. Although he already left the show, he had made brief appearances in the later episodes.
