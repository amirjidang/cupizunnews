James Norton Imogen Poots Split: Are James Norton and Imogen Poots still together?

Publish date: 2024-08-08

James Norton and Imogen Poots are still together and the couple has been engaged since 2022.

James Norton has been in a relationship with actress Imogen Poots since 2018. James Norton appeared in Amy Herzog’s Belleville at the Donmar Warehouse Theatre between December 2017 and February 2018.

James Norton played John Brooke in the 2019 film adaptation of Louisa May Alcott’s Little Women, directed by Greta Gerwig. In 2015, he was appointed as a trustee of the Royal Theatrical Support Trust.

James Norton appeared in the film An Education in 2009 and was a member of the original cast of Posh at the Royal Court Theatre in 2010. He also starred in That Face at the Crucible Theatre in Sheffield and played Captain Stanhope in the First World War drama Journey’s End in 2011.

In 2016, James Norton appeared in the London West End production of Bug by Tracy Letts, and in an episode of the anthology series Black Mirror called “Nosedive.”
