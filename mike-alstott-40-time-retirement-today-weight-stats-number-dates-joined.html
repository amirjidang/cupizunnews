Mike Alstott 40 Time, Retirement, Today, Weight, Stats, Number, Dates Joined

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Mike Alstott is an American former professional football player in the National Football League.

He played collegiate football at Purdue and was drafted by the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in the second round of the 1996 NFL Draft.

Alstott spent his entire 12-year NFL career with the team from 1996 to 2007.

Mike Alstott 40 Time

At the 1996 NFL draft combine, Alstott ran the 40-yard dash in 4.66 seconds.

Mike Alstott Retirement

Mike Alstott retired in January 2008 due to a neck injury.

Mike Alstott Today

In December 2022, Mike Alstott stepped down as head football coach at Northside Christian after 11 seasons. He is currently running the Mike Alstott Family Foundation which he established with his family in the 2007 off-season.

Mike Alstott Weight

Mike Alstott weighs 248 pounds (112 kg).

Mike Alstott Stats

Mike Alstott played 12 seasons for the Buccaneers. He had 1,359 carries for 5,088 yards, 305 catches for 2,284 yards and scored 71 touchdowns. He was selected to play in 6 Pro Bowls and won 1 championship.

Mike Alstott Number

Mike Alstott wore the No. 40 jersey for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

Mike Alstott Dates Joined

Mike Alstott joined the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in 1996.
