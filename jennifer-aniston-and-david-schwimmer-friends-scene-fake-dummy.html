What Happened Between Jennifer Aniston And David Schwimmer?

Forever, Jennifer Aniston and David Schwimmer will be linked to one another, given their portrayal of Ross and Rachel on 'Friends' for a decade.

To this very day, the 'Friends' couple faces dating rumors, something Aniston laughs off.

"I was getting a couple of texts from people saying, 'I thought you were on a break, LOL.' And I kept saying, 'What are you talking about?' And then I went online to see what was happening, and I was like, 'That is the funniest rumor that I never heard that got shot down in the quickest amount of time.'"

However, the two would in fact admit that there was a bit of truth to the rumors during the earlier seasons. According to the 'Friends' duo, they crushed on each other during the earlier seasons.

"At some point, we were both crushing hard on each other," Schwimmer continued. "But it was like two ships passing, because one of us was always in a relationship. And we never crossed that boundary. We respected that."

Off camera, the two got along extremely well. However, in this instance, Aniston was not happy with Schwimmer's antics. Let's take a look at how it all went down.

Jennifer Aniston Wasn't Happy With Schwimmer's On-Screen Prank

The episode in question aired way back in February of 2001. 'The One with the Truth About London' took place in season 7, episode 16. The main plot surrounded how Monica and Chandler really hooked up in London, though one of the other storylines saw Rachel try to become the cool aunt alongside Ben.

In the episode, Ross wasn't happy with Rachel's approach, as she was teaching Ben various pranks - that he would later test out on his dad.

David Schwimmer actually directed the episode. He decided to have some fun during the final scene, airing a real prank that actually aired. The moment was completely unscripted, as Ross used a fake dummy to throw down the stairs, just as Rachel was trying to catch up to them.

Aniston was not told about the moment, which made her reaction that much greater. It turns out, she was none too pleased with not being told, and we can see the anger on her face after the moment took place. Schwimmer on the other hand was all laughs, along with the audience who also had a blast with the moment.

What did the fans think of the scene? Well, it should come as a surprise that they completely loved it.
