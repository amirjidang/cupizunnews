Killer Mike &amp; El-P Make Fans Salivate Over 'Run The Jewels 3'

Publish date: 2024-08-23

As fans of Run The Jewels continue to impatiently wait for their third studio album, Killer Mike & El-P eased the pain of their nearly two-year wait with a new preview of Run The Jewels 3.

Last night, Killer Mike took to Instagram to reveal a new sound from the album with the caption “#RTJ3.”

#RTJ3

A video posted by Killer Mike (@killermike) on Feb 11, 2016 at 5:33pm PST

This comes as the third glimpse into their forthcoming LP Back in May of 2015, Mike & El-P confirmed that their busy touring schedule kept them from releasing RTJ3. El-P then offered a brief clip of what’s in store for the album.

Then last month, after fans were forced to accept Meow The Jewels as their RTJ fix, El-P let go of another 15-second clip from the album, which reignited the anticipation for the duo’s upcoming project and made it stronger than ever.

There’s no confirmed release date for the album yet.
