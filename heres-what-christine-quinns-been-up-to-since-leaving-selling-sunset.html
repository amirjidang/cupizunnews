Here's What Christine Quinn's Been Up To Since Leaving Selling Sunset

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Popular reality series Selling Sunset has managed to surpass viewers' expectations and break viewing records. From its elite real estate agents to their luxurious lifestyles, fashion statements, and major dramatic moments, it's no wonder why Netflix renewed the show for a sixth and seventh season and a spinoff Selling the OC is already underway.

RELATED: Why Christine Quinn Is Feuding With Everyone On Selling Sunset

One particular breakout star has stood out in the first five seasons of the show, which is none other than Christine Quinn. Unfortunately, Christine will not be returning to the Oppenheim Group when season 6 premieres this summer, but will still be a part of the show even though she didn't appear at the season 5 reunion.

From her sassy and boss bitch vibes to fashion killer looks, this blonde bombshell appears not to be leaving the spotlight anytime soon. So here's what the reality star has been up to since her departure from the company.

9 Christine Walked The Star-studded Balenciaga Runway In Paris

Christine recently walked the star-studded Balenciaga couture show which took place in Paris, alongside A-listers like Kim Kardashian, Nicole Kidman, Dua Lipa, Bella Hadid, and runway queen Naomi Campbell. She spoke about how excited and honored she was, having been selected to be a part of the show. Undeniably, Christine is leaving her mark on the catwalk.

8 She Launched Her Book

For viewers who have followed the fabulousness and charisma of Christine as she unapologetically carried herself on the show, Christine has launched her book, How to Be a Boss Bitch, a guide to unleashing the most bold and confident version of yourself. While promoting her book Christine said, "Season 1 came out and the No. 1 DM and message people were asking me wasn't about fashion of hair or beauty. It was actually about confidence and that's where the idea really came from."

7 She Is Spending Time Hanging Out With Fashion's Finest

From late night catwalks in Paris, to front row sitting at top fashion shows, to taking pictures with stars like Anna Wintour, Kanye West, Euphoria star Alexa Demie, Meg Thee Stallion, Christine is having the time of her life. Her Instagram page says it all as she proves once again that she's not just a reality star, but so much more.

6 She Made The Cover Of Grazia UK…. Again

The show-stopping star recently posted her photo on the cover of Grazia UK. In her Instagram post she said, "So honored to be on this month's cover of Grazia UK for the second time. We shot 10 looks over 7+ hours. I am so grateful to the entire team that made this possible, all in the name of fashion. I love you always and forever London." Just as expected, the fashion queen is a sight to behold.

5 Christine Is Influencing More Than Ever Before

Christine has no doubt had a busy year. In addition to being a real estate agent, model, reality star, and fashion entrepreneur, her social media contains influencing posts, from #AngryBirdsJourney, to #Samsung and their different Galaxy models. If there is one thing about Christine, she is going to get the bag.

4 She Launched Her Real Estate Company With Her Husband

Christine is everywhere and making moves. In April 2022, she announced the launch of her real estate company with Tech entrepreneur husband Christian Dumontet called RealOpen. According to her, this new company will "revolutionize" the housing market." Her company is introducing new technological dynamism, making it possible to purchase properties via cryptocurrency. Now, not only will Christine feel like a boss bitch, she will be the boss bitch, and fans are ready for this new venture.

3 Christine Is Focusing On Her Shoe Collection With ShoeDazzle

Last year, Christine announced a collaboration with ShoeDazzle on her "Holiday Sparkle" shoe collection, which was reflective of her over-the-top, sparkly, and glamorous fashion style, consisting of pumps, sandals and boots. Now, she has teamed up with the shoe brand for her second curated collection for summer, which she announced in April.

2 She's Living Her Rich Married Life With Her Rich Husband

Christine has stated time and time again that she was going to marry rich. Having already made her own money, she has been very vocal about being financially independent and working very hard for her coins. Now, even a rich husband isn't stopping her as she is still putting in the work from and putting her flamboyant life on display for her over 3 million followers.

1 Christine Is Selling Miami Now

Although it has been confirmed by Christine that although she has parted ways with the Oppenheim Group to focus on her own company with husband, she will still very much be a part of the show. However, the star also stated in an Instagram post, "She Selling Miami now", with some of her company's listings in Miami on their website.
