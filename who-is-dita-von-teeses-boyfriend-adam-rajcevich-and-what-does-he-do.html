Who Is Dita Von Teese's Boyfriend Adam Rajcevich, And What Does He Do?

Dita Von Teese, who was previously married to Marilyn Manson, has been in a long relationship with Adam Rajcevich. Apparently, her boyfriend isn’t on any social media platforms and remains private about his personal life, so many fans wonder who really Adam is and what he does for a living.

Who Is Adam Rajcevich And What Does He Do?

Adam, who is best known as the boyfriend of the famous burlesque dancer and celebrity Dita Von Teese, has kept his personal life very secret from all media attention. As for his profile, he has next to no data referenced on the web. He has not been found either on any social media sites including Instagram. He hasn’t even uncovered his parent’s and family subtleties.

As for his job, Adam currently works a visual designer at the Walt Disney Company. It looks like the graphic designer designs different clothes for the company. While there is no specific information about his net worth, his girlfriend Dita is undeniably pretty rich. The American actress has a whooping estimated net worth of $16 million.

Are Adam And Dita Tying The Knot?

Adam and Dita, who have been dating since August 2014, seem to be very happy with each other. Although they keep their love life private, the model still shares some tidbits about their relationship. In an interview, she revealed that she’s been very happy with him but tying the knots is not one of the options for her at the moment – stating that she would rather show her commitment in another way.

The 48-year-old beauty, who was married to a famous rock star before, took the opportunity to explain why she doesn’t want to marry again. She said, “I felt like I already had my big showbiz wedding thing – but maybe that was because I married a rock star. What I learned from my last engagements and marriage is that the most romantic part is being engaged and telling someone you want to be with them for the rest of your life.”

Further explaining her reason, she added, “So I thought about being perpetually engaged. I’m not engaged yet. But Adam and I have talked about it, not having a big wedding and just a long engagement. He agrees and is like, ‘Alright yeah, I’m into that.’”

Do They Have Plans of Having Children?

Speaking about not having children, Dita admitted, “At this stage in life, especially considering the state of the world, I don’t feel regret. Oprah Winfrey didn’t have children – I keep going back to that fact! I love to travel, I love being free, I love showbiz.” Aside from a lot of celebrities who absolutely love Oprah, the model seems to be fond of her too.

Dita further explained, “I feel like I have a lot of love in my life with my pets, my family, my boyfriend and my friends. I’ve made my decisions and I have to live with them regardless.” Despite her decision, the American celebrity has admitted that she envied her friends and other people she knows that have lovely adult children. But then, she said that her sister has always reassured her.

She shared, “I have nephews and my sister has always said, ‘You’re their godmother too.’ But I don’t think I’ll regret it. I’ll probably be that old lady with cats, dogs, rabbits and birds.” Will the couple be having kids in the future? That's for them to decide!
