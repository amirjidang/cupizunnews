Only Murders In The Building is a comedy-drama series involving three strangers who are obsessed with true crime and wind up in an actual case. The series received accolades and critical acclaim for its comedic approach to fictional crime, and recognition for cast performances, including the chemistry between cast members. From Steve Martin, to Selena Gomez, Martin Short, Amy Ryan, and more, here's the cast of Only Murders In The Building ranked by net worth!

The first episode of Only Murders In The Building streamed on Hulu on August 31, 2021 and a third season was renewed in July 2022. The series has received awards and nominations, including the People's Choice Award for the Comedy TV Star of the Year, and more. The series was written by Steve Martin, John Robert Hoffman, Madeleine George, Kristin Newman, Ben Smith, and Ben Philippe, including an all-star cast listed below by net worth.
