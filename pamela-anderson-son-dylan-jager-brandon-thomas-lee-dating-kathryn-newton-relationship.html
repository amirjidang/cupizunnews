The Truth About Kathryn Newton's Relationship With Brandon Thomas And Dylan Jagger Lee

In 2019, there were rumors about Kathryn Newton dating Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee's first son Brandon Thomas Lee. These speculations about their relationship started circulating after they worked together on a project and people began noticing them spending time together. Newton's career has been growing steadily since she was just five years old.

Although Newton is constantly in the public eye, she has been very private about her romantic life. Even her social media posts do not suggest that she is dating, has a boyfriend, or is planning to get married. She has however been the subject of dating rumors over the years, particularly with Brandon Thomas Lee.

Kathryn Newton Relationship With Dylan Jager And Brandon Thomas Lee

A source told E! News that Lee and the Ant-Man star Newton were spending time together and genuinely having a great time in each other's company. They were spotted together in Santa Monica, California, having a meal at Giorgio Baldi restaurant.

Lee was also spotted in Beverly Hills another time with Newton where the two had a lovely romantic dinner at a famous restaurant. Lee was dressed in a big t-shirt and army green cargo pants with black Converse sneakers.

Newton, on the other hand, was putting on a black top paired with a black and white knitted sweater on light blue jeans, and platform sandals. A source at the restaurant also shared with People that they were having a fantastic time, laughing together and enjoying each other's company. The source went as far as describing what they had eaten at the dinner.

Did Kathryn Newton Actually Date Brandon Lee?

In a conversation with Us Weekly in 2019, Brandon Lee denied the rumors that he and Newton were dating and clarified they were simply good friends. He explained that they both led hectic lives which made it difficult to spend much time together. He also mentioned that they were simply friends that get to see each other now and then.

On the other hand, Newton chose not to confirm or deny the speculation surrounding her relationship with Lee during an exclusive interview with Us Weekly at the 2020 SAG Awards.

Instead, she shifted the focus to discussing her role in a movie exclaiming, "Oh my god! Okay, first of all, Chrome Hearts is the coolest, and it's so cool to be part of that." However, she only had good things to say about Lee, "Brandon is the best. We've been friends since high school, so to get to do that with him was so much fun." Therefore, the rumours claiming they were in a romantic relationship might just be speculations after all.

Brandon Thomas Lee's Dating History

Apart from being Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee’s son, Brandon Thomas Lee has made a name for himself as an actor and model, particularly because of his attractive appearance and charming personality even though he is yet to make big bucks like his famous mum.

Being a celebrity has opened his personal and romantic life up to public curiosity and there have been numerous speculations about Lee’s romantic life. This started in 2015 when rumors circulated about his relationship with Bella Thorne. However, their relationship was short-lived, and Lee soon started dating Raina Lawson that same year.

After staying single for a period in 2016, Lee got into a relationship with Pyper Smith, which lasted about a year and in 2020, he started dating Daniella Beckerman. Currently, Lee is dating Lily Easton who is seemingly close to his parents. The two have been a couple since 2021 and recently celebrated their anniversary in November 2022.

Is Kathryn Newton Currently Dating Anyone?

In a 2019 interview with Cosmopolitan, Kathryn Newton, who was 22 years old at the time, shared that she had never been in a romantic relationship. She mentioned this while discussing her experience on the set of The Society. Newton had also expressed her lack of interest in dating fellow actors.

She shared her preference to remain single saying, "It's a bummer. Or is it a bummer? I've seen many girls my age get their hearts broken." She went on to reveal Emma Roberts once tried to set her up with a few actors she thought would be a good match. However, none of them interested Newton because she has no desire to date anyone from the industry.

Inside Kathryn Newton’s Personal Life And Acting Career

While she keeps her romantic life quite private, the actress is pretty famous for her talent and has starred in major movies and TV shows. She gained significant career momentum during her teenage years when she landed roles in TV shows like "Gary Unmarried" which aired from 2008 to 2010 and "Supernatural," leading up to her breakthrough in Big Little Lies.

After that, she appeared in acclaimed movies like Lady Bird and BBC's Little Women adaptation. In 2019, she starred in Detective Pikachu and the popular Netflix series, The Society. In 2023, Newton co-starred as Cassie Lang in Marvel’s Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania.

Newton is not only captivating audiences on screen but also finding joy in different things in her personal life. She is a dog lover just like Selena Gomez and showcases her adorable dogs on Instagram.

In an exclusive interview with Vogue, Newton gushes about the happiness her dogs bring, as waking up to their presence is her favorite part of the day. She admitted to feeling like a spectacle as passersby's gazes followed her but realized her cute poodles were the ones stealing the limelight. Even when she hits the golf course, which she does at least once a week, she revealed that her poodles are by her side, adding a touch of charm.

As much as fans and critics would love to know a lot about Newton’s romantic life, it is clear she has intentionally kept that part of her life out of the public eye. One thing that is certain is that Newton is not in a romantic relationship with Lee as he has been confirmed to be in a serious relationship with Lily Easton.
