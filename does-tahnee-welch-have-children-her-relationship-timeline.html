Does Tahnee Welch Have Children? Her Relationship Timeline

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Amid the recent news of Raquel Welch’s passing at the age of 82, many people might be interested in learning whether her daughter, Latanne “Tahnee” Welch, ever married or who she came closest to tying the knot with.

Tahnee was born on December 26th, 1961, and is now 61 years old. Her film career started with a starring role in George P Coasmatos’ directorial debut, Sin (also known as The Beloved and Restless), alongside Richard Johnson.

Though Tahnee has not been married, she has had several relationships that have made headlines over the years. This includes her relationship with British actor Jared Harris, born just months prior to Tahnee, who goes by notable roles in Mad Men (Lane Pryce), The Crown (King George VI), The Expanse (Anderson Dawes) and more recently Morbius (Emil Nicholas).

Harris also played Valery Legasov in 2019’s Chernobyl series. He and Tahnee began dating in 1991 and eventually split five years later without marrying.

After her separation from Harris, Tahnee was romantically involved with Brooklyn-born actor Luca Palanca for some time; they met while working together at Joe & Joe’s Pizzeria.

However, even though their relationship did not lead to marriage, it received its fair share of attention since everyone knew Tahnee was Raquel Welch’s daughter. 

In 2008, Palanca wed Tammy Pescatelli and had a child shortly after their nuptials.

All in all, Latanne “Tahnee” Welch has never been married nor does she have any children that we know of, the closest thing to a husband being Jared Harris, who she dated briefly during the 1990s, which makes her mother, Raquel Welch’s only biological child without any progeny.

Tahnee And Jared Harris Relationship 

Tahnee Welch and Jared Harris’s relationship ran from 1991 until 1996; it is unclear what led to the dissolution of their relationship, though they never married.

Harris has been known for his roles in major film and television series over the years, including Mad Men (Lane Pryce), The Crown (King George VI), The Expanse (Anderson Dawes), Morbius (Emil Nicholas) and Chernobyl (Valery Legasov). During the time of their romantic involvement, both of them were still relatively unknown actors.

Raquel Welch was reported as having been fond of Harris, describing him as “a kind, gentle soul.” It is reported that she had high hopes for a potential marriage between her daughter and Harris but, despite lasting five years, the two split without marrying.
