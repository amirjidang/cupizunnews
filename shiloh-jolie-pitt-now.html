Shiloh Jolie-Pitt Has Grown Up as a Stylish Icon After Changing Her Looks

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt’s tomboyish teenage girl, Shiloh Jolie-Pitt has been celebrated as an LGBT icon the world over and was recognized on sporting denim cut-offs, pierced ears, and long hair tied up in a bun on a rare new public appearance.

It is difficult to believe that it has been this long since Angelina Jolie and ex-husband Brad Pitt embraced their first biological child into the world, but Shiloh Nouvel Jolie-Pitt turned 15 years old on May 27 of this year! Does not it just seem like yesterday that Brangelina debuted their beloved newborn on the cover of People Magazine?

It seems Shiloh Jolie-Pitt has undergone quite the makeover while in lockdown if these new photos are anything to go by. Sporting denim cut-offs, pierced ears, and long locks tied up in a bun, it’s a far cry from the gender-bending tomboy dressing Shiloh has worn over the years.

The 15-year-old made a rare public appearance when she was snapped visiting an Ethiopian design boutique in Los Angeles alongside mum Angelina Jolie and 16-year-old sister Zaharaon on January 8.

Wearing a black hoodie and denim cut-off shorts along with black Converse trainers, Shiloh has also shot up in the height department. Her unusual look could even be considered conventional or stereotypical, at least compared to her old style.

Angelina said Vanity Fair in a 2010 interview that Shiloh has been exploring gender since age three. “She wants to be a boy,” Jolie said. “So we had to cut her hair. She likes to wear boy clothes. She thinks she’s one of her brothers.” In a 2008 interview with Oprah Winfrey, Brad opened up about raising Shiloh: “She only wants to be called John. John or Peter. It’s a Peter Pan thing. And then I’ll say, ‘John, would you like some orange juice?’ And she goes, ‘No!’”

Since then, countless fans have widely referred to Shiloh as John, with a whole Wikipedia page and social fan pages popping up all over the internet. In fact, a hunt on Google Trends reveals that online users are exploring both “Shiloh” and “John” pretty equally.

No matter what name Shiloh likes to go by or what clothes to wear, Angelina and Brad have done nothing but welcome their kiddo’s uniqueness. “I would never be the kind of parent to force somebody to be something they are not,” the Maleficent star told Reuters. “I think that is just bad parenting.”

Angelina and Brad raised Shiloh together until they got divorced in 2016. Since then, they have been confused in a tumultuous custody battle, with the latest update in May 2021 when the Fight Club actor was granted joint custody.

Though it’s been a long road and their children have “been through a lot,” the Those Who Wish Me Dead actress said her kids’ courage her. “I learn from their strength,” Angelina told Harper’s Bazaar in 2019. “We can’t prevent them from experiencing pain, heartache, physical pain, and loss. But we can teach them to live better through it.”

Over the years, Shiloh has also created a buzz with her androgynous, bold chic style. Her parents always supported her in dressing the way she wanted. She would wear casual separates as a child – polo shirts, jackets, blazers, shorts, dark trousers, jeans, and oversized tracksuits.

When Shiloh’s parents would take her to red carpet premieres and events, she skipped gowns and dresses in favor of suits similar to what her dad wore.

So it’s no shock that followers of the superstar family have seen the unexpected change in Shiloh’s style. In any case, Shiloh’s had a massive growth spurt and seems to be already heightening over her mother.

Facts About Shiloh Jolie-Pitt

In 2006, Shiloh Jolie-Pitt first commanded international headlines as the firstborn of America’s most star-studded couple. Below are some of the interesting facts about her.

1. Shiloh was not born in the United States

Although Shiloh has been brought up in the United States, she was born in Swakopmund, Namibia. Angelina Jolie spent a lot of time in Africa as a UN refugee ambassador. She decided to give birth in Namibia after falling in love with the sparsely-populated country’s beautiful countryside. Although many of Namibia’s two million citizens live in poverty and don’t have access to quality health care, Jolie gave birth in a hospital outfitted with modern equipment to deliver baby Shiloh safely.

2. Shiloh is a world record holder

It might be an unknown claim to fame. But in 2006, Shiloh Jolie-Pitt became the youngest person ever to be immortalized in wax at Madame Tussauds, the famous London wax museum. The lookalike doll of Shiloh represents her at just two months old, as she’s held in the arms of Angelina and Brad. In 2016, Madame Tussauds separated the wax version of “Brangelina” to reflect the couple’s recently announced divorce.

3. She has a movie credit

Although young, Shiloh Jolie-Pitt is already displaying some interest in the entertainment world. In 2016, Shiloh had a small part in the hit animated film Kung Fu Panda 3. Alongside siblings Pax, Zahara and Knox, Shiloh recorded some animal noises that were used in the movie. According to their mother, the kids had a blast getting in the studio and channeling their inner panda. At this rate, Shiloh will have more film credits than mum and dad combined!

4. She’s no new to the red carpet

Although Jolie and Pitt like to prioritize their family’s privacy, that has not prevented them from inviting their children onto the red carpet from time to time. And Shiloh has rarely passed up an opportunity to welcome the glitz and glamour of a Hollywood premiere. She recently accompanied her mum and dad on the red carpet for the premiere of Disney’s Dumbo.

5. She has some famous friends

It is not just her family that’s famous. Shiloh Jolie-Pitt also owns a star-studded social circle. Most recently, she was observed getting pizza with her sister Zahara and Stranger Things star Millie Bobby Brown. The image was posted to Instagram and revealed the three young celebs hanging out at 11 pm. Although it might have been past their bedtime, these young celebrities were under the watchful eye of Millie Bobby Brown’s mum.

Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt are a little unique from average Hollywood stars. They prefer to give back and speak out – and it’s clear they are introducing those same qualities in their children. So we witness that Shiloh Jolie-Pitt is already moving to the beat of her drum, and we can’t wait to see what she does next.
