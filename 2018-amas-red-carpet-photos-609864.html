Cardi B, J. Lo And More Hit The 2018 AMAs Red Carpet

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Cardi B and Offset turned the 2018 American Music Awards red carpet into a mini date night. The couple showed up to the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles Tuesday ( Oct. 9) with coordinating looks and lots of PDA.

The “I Like It” rapper stole the show in a floral Dolce & Gabanna gown and matching headpiece, while Offset rocked a cranberry-colored suit. And they weren’t the only music couple showing off their love on the carpet. Ty Dolla Sign and his girlfriend, Lauren Jauregui, also posed for pics in complimenting outfits.

Bold looks were an unofficial theme for the night, from monochromatic pantsuits to pops of color, and metallics.

AMAs host Tracee Ellis Ross is one fashion star who doesn’t mind stepping outside the box. The Black-ish actress sported a chic monochromatic pants suit on the carpet, while Chloe x Hall looked absolutely heavenly in matching gowns.
