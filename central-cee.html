Who is Central Cee?

Oakley Cesar-Su was born in Ladbroke, London, on June 4, 1998. He began his music career at 14 after a friend introduced him to the recording studio. He adopted a stage name, Central Cee.

He earned widespread recognition with successful singles’ Day in the Life’ and ‘Loading’ in 2020. He released his debut mixtape, ‘Wild West,’ on March 12, 2021, which debuted at number two on the UK Albums chart. His second mixtape, ‘23,’ dropped on February 25, 2022, and ranked top of the UK Albums Chart.

His single ‘Doja’ became the most streamed UK rap song on Spotify and ranked number two on the UK Singles Chart. He released his debut EP ‘No More Leaks’ in October 2022. He released the track ‘Sprinter’ earlier this year in June 2023, along with artist Dave, which became his first song to be a UK Number-one single and held the position for ten weeks.
