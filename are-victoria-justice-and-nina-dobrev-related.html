Here's Why Fans Think Victoria Justice And Nina Dobrev Are Related

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Highlights

  • Watchers of The Vampire Diaries noticed Nina Dobrev closely resembles Victoria Justice - even Wikipedia once confused the two.
  • Victoria Justice doesn't mind the mix-up though. Actually, she considers it a huge compliment.
  • Despite their similarities, Nina Dobrev and Victoria Justice are not related, coming from very different backgrounds.

For viewers who sat down to watch CW's The Vampire Diaries when it debuted in 2009 without knowing who the cast members were, that first episode was a trip. Nina Dobrev, who played the lead on the show (Elena Gilbert), looked really familiar.

But watching the show's credits, it became clear that Nina Dobrev was the headlining act.

The thing was, she looked so much like another Hollywood actress, even Wikipedia once got confused. Yep, there's a broken link to an article about CW casting Victoria Justice. Only, that never happened.

Honestly, it's a relief that someone finally said it: Victoria Justice and Nina Dobrev look a lot alike.

Victoria Justice Admits To Getting Mistaken For Nina Dobrev

Victoria Justice thinks getting mistaken for Nina Dobrev is a huge compliment

Fans aren't the only ones who've noticed the similarity between Victoria and Nina. Victoria told MTV that people often mistake her for Nina in everyday life, explaining, "Dude! You don't understand, I get this every single day... Everyone always confuses me for her." So it wasn't surprising when MTV journalists asked for her thoughts on one red carpet.

Of course, Victoria notes, it's a complete compliment. And upon meeting, the pair 'hugged it out.' The ladies are also close in age; Victoria is 31 and Nina is 35. So, they could be sisters.

Nina Dobrev And Victoria Justice Are NOT Related

Victoria Justice and Nina Dobrev have very different backgrounds and grew up in different locales

Except, it's highly unlikely that they're actually related, even distantly. Victoria Justice's background is a mix of English, German, Irish, and Puerto Rican ancestry, and she grew up in Florida before moving to California. Nina Dobrev, however, was born in Bulgaria and moved to Toronto, Canada at age two.

In fact, Nina's start as an actress came from a role on Degrassi: The Next Generation. That's basically the equivalent of Victoria Justice's Hollywood beginnings on Zoe: 101, right?

The ladies both appeared at the Teen Choice Awards in 2015, where Victoria presented Nina with her award for Choice TV Actress, Sci-Fi/Fantasy.

Victoria Justice And Nina Dobren Have More In Common Still

Nina Dobrev and Victoria Justice are philanthropists and have a similar net worth
