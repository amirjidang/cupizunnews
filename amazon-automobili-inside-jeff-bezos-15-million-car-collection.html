Inside Jeff Bezos' $15 Million Car Collection

Being one of the richest men globally, Jeff Bezos has undoubtedly become a topic of conversation in the past few years. While the Founder of Amazon is known to have a relatively low-key lifestyle, he certainly requires speed taking in his giant car collection. With a net worth of an astonishing $195 billion, Bezos spent a comparatively small fortune to add some supercars to his garage. Not only does his collection feature some of the fastest cars in the world, but they are also rare in number, which makes them all the more valuable.

From Italian brands and manufacturers like Lamborghini and Ferrari to Lebanese production brands such as W Motors and Swedish company Koenigsegg, Jeff Bezos has acquired a diverse portfolio of cars, each unique from the other. Let’s take a look inside the billionaire’s garage and check out the cars worth $15 million.

Lamborghini Veneno ($5,000,000)

Knocking down the racing barrier, Lamborghini celebrated fifty years of its moniker in style when it introduced the Veneno in 2013. Costing $5 million, the Veneno is a highly exclusive car that combines fifty years of technology and craftsmanship in one vehicle. Bold, futuristic, and powerful, it features a 6.5-liter V12 engine that provides a 750 HP output with a top speed of 220.5 MPH. Lamborghini reported that it reaches 0 to 62 MPH in just 2.7 seconds with its 7-speed ISR transmission. A rare car, only 13 Veneno and Veneno Roadster models are available worldwide.

Bugatti Veyron Mansory ($3,500,000)

A limited-edition Bugatti, the Veyron Mansory is designed with an iconic V shape that gives the model the name Vivere. The car is a combination of a sheer fast-driving experience and refined luxury. Dubbed as Bezos’ favorite car, only 2 Mansory cars have been manufactured to date. As mentioned by Car Buzz, the car is equipped with a quad-turbocharged 8.0-liter W16 engine and unique customization. As one of the most seminal cars of the 21st century, Bugatti has only produced 240 variants of the Veyron. The supercar purchased by Bezos costs a whopping $3.5 million.

Lykan Hypersport ($4,000,000)

Lykan Hypersport is the first supercar created by W Motors featured in the hit action movie Fast 7. The car has an angular styling and timeless look with a patented reverse dihedral door system. Only seven units have been produced to date, making it another exclusive car in Jeff Bezos’ collection. The Lykan also has the first holographic mid-air display globally with 440-diamond encrusted LED lights. The boxer-type twin-turbocharged engine provides 780 HP and 708 lb-ft of torque. The car prances from 0 to 62 MPH in merely 2.9 seconds, as noted by W Motors. A rare and expensive model, the Lykan Hypersport comes with a $4 million price tag.

Ferrari Pininfarina Sergio ($3,000,000)

The collaboration between Ferrari and Pininfarina, the Ferrari Pininfarina Sergio, was first debuted at the 2013 Geneve Motor Show, a tribute to Sergio Pininfarina. According to Motor 1, the car's production was kept limited to only six cars; hence the car had a buying price of a whopping $3 million. It is powered by a 4.5-liter V8 that provides an output of 605 HP and 398 lb-ft of torque. It runs from 0 to 62 MPH in only 3 seconds. The Euro-spec Sergio has Alcantara and black leather interior that oozes luxury and style. With only six produced, one was shipped to the United Arab Emirates, and three were sent to the United States. One was purchased by a gentleman in Japan, while someone in Switzerland acquired the final model.

Koenigsegg CCXR Trevita ($5,000,000)

With only two Koenigsegg CCXR Trevita ever built, it is the rarest car in Jeff Bezos’ garage. The supercar was built to be made street legal in the United States. One car was bought by Bezos, while boxer Floyd Mayweather purchased the other one. Trevita comes from the Swedish word Three Whites, which denotes the carbon fiber bodywork and diamond weave. The powerful 4.8-liter V8 delivers 1,018 HP through the Xtrac Super Synchro 6-Speed transmission. It reaches 0 to 60 MPH in 2.9 seconds with a top speed of 250 MPH. The extremely rare car was purchased for $5 million.

Honda Accord (Priceless)

A priceless car in the Jeff Bezos garage is the 1997 Honda Accord, one of his earliest purchases. The billionaire was spotted driving the car up until 2013. Accord has been the best-selling car in America, creating a benchmark for regular retail sales. The car comes with a powerful V6 engine that gives an output of 130 HP and 139 lb-ft of torque. The five-seater car is available in 5-speed and 4-speed automatic transmission. In 1997, the Honda Accord cost upwards of $18,585.

From owning old classic cars that bring nostalgia to the rarest cars the world has ever seen, Jeff Bezos has a diverse collection that costs way over $15 million. While the billionaire enjoys simple things in life, he does have a special place in his garage for expensive supercars. As his net worth remains ever-growing, the billionaire garage will have more cars added to the stunning collection in years to come.
