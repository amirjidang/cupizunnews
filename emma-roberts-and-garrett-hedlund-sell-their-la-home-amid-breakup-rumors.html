Emma Roberts And Garrett Hedlund Sell Their LA Home, Amid Breakup Rumors

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Emma Roberts and Garrett Hedlund are in the process of getting rid of their house in the neighborhood of Los Angeles listed for a little under $2 million following rumors of their split. The couple who began dating in March 2019 reportedly have called it quits and decided to sell their humble abode. However, it was also reported that Emma Roberts already purchased a place of her own, a new home in Los Angeles she bought for $3.7 million.

The house features three bedrooms with four baths that sits on about 7,300 square feet of property located on the hillside of Los Feliz. The estate is a Spanish-style building constructed as early as 1926 and later had some minor reconstructions in 2001. The property gives a vibe of combination of classic aesthetics with a blend of modern design.

The three bedrooms of the house were all located upstairs, covering 2,500 square feet of the ground. Two bedrooms share one full bathroom in the house that can easily function as a guest room. The property was assumed to have been bought off-market since the estate was not advertised in the public. It is also not clear when the couple bought the property but they put it on sale this week.

The Scream Queen actress was already ready to move on with a new home she bought and it seems that there is no turning back as the listing of their home is Zillow says that it is already in contingency. Once a property is already in contingency, an offer has already been made and accepted, but certain requirements are needed to be made for the sale to be final.

Emma Roberts and Garrett Hedlund just welcomed their son Rhodes Robert in December 2020. Despite sharing this sweet milestone in their life, it seems that the two have separated ways as the pair continues to spark major speculations of their breakup. The two seemed perfect initially as they started as friends and later developed feelings for each other and decided to give their relationship a try. News about their pregnancy broke in June 2020 and insider source said that the pair was surprised and happy to start a new family. The A-list pair was said to be well adjusted to the parenthood and Hedlund was even gushing over his special baby boy, describing his son as an angel.
