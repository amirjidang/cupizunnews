Travis Kelce's Ex Slams Swifties For Attacking Her After She Allegedly Bashed Taylor Swift's Music

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Highlights

  • Kayla Nicole received backlash from overly intense Taylor Swift fans for supposedly bashing the singer's music, despite not directly mentioning her.
  • The model clapped back at "swifties" on social media, defending herself and asking for the hate to stop, receiving mostly supportive comments.
  • Despite the drama, Kayla Nicole and Travis Kelce's past relationship seems to have ended amicably, and she still maintains a good relationship with the Kelce family.

Much like Taylor Swift was coming out of a six-year committed relationship with Joe Alwyn, Travis Kelce had also ended a long relationship shortly before they got together. He dated reporter and model Kayla Nicole for five years, and despite their breakup, it doesn't seem there's any animosity between them or between her and Taylor Swift.

The same cannot be said about the swifties. Taylor Swift's fans have been hostile towards Kayla for a while now, interpreting anything she does or says as an attack on their favorite singer, and it seems the model finally had enough and decided to respond to swifties who were leaving mean comments on her social media.

Kayla Nicole Slammed Swifties And Asked Them To Leave Her Alone

Sometimes fans can get a little too intense about their love for an artist, and swifties are no exception. Apparently, Kayla Nicole, Travis Kelce's ex-girlfriend, was on the receiving end of attacks from Taylor Swift fans, who felt she had slammed Taylor's music.

While she's generally not that outspoken about her problems, it seems the online bashing got a little too much for the model, which is understandable, and she clapped back at swifties.

"I’m unproblematic. Mind my business. Never respond to the constant vitriol. Solid as they come & don’t expect a pat on the back for it either," she defended herself. "Just know everyone has a breaking point & would love for 'yall' (because you know exactly who you are) to leave me alone."

She received, mostly, a lot of support from the comments on her post, and there were even some swifties who sent their love and asked other fans to stop hating on Kayla Nicole.

"As a lifelong swiftie and a big fan of yours, I feel like I need to apologize on their behalf for how disgusting they are to you. you don’t deserve it at all and please know there are some of us who also love and respect you and wish they would just leave you alone," one user wrote, and was praised by other swifties.

Kayla's Alleged Bashing Of Taylor Swift's Music

There was always a certain hostility towards Kayla Nicole for simply being Travis Kelce's ex-girlfriend, but it noticeably ramped up after a particular tweet that swifties interpreted as her trashing Taylor's music.

"There’s just no way I can fully digest all these new albums. I’m still on Cowboy Carter," she wrote the day before Taylor Swift released her new album, and a lot of people thought she was calling The Tortured Poets Department irrelevant.

"Girl bye... always throwing some sneaky little shade but don't have the guts to just come out and say it. Insecure and extremely immature!!" a user wrote in reply.
