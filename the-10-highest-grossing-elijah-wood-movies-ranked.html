Ladies and gentlemen, hold on to your hobbit feet and get ready for a cinematic journey through the Elijah Wood's illustrious career. From Middle-earth to the streets of Los Angeles, this list will take you on a whirlwind adventure through some of the most memorable and successful films of the past few decades. With his piercing blue eyes and undeniable talent, Elijah Wood has captivated audiences time and time again, and these movies are proof of that. So, grab some popcorn and settle in as we count down the top 10 Elijah Wood movies, ranked by their impressive box office earnings. Are you ready to embark on this cinematic journey? Let's go!

The Adventures of Huck Finn - 1993 ($24 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Elijah Wood starred as the mischievous and resourceful Huck Finn in this classic adaptation of Mark Twain's novel. With his natural talent and infectious energy, Wood captured the spirit of the character, earning critical praise and helping the film gross $24 million worldwide. His portrayal of the mischievous and resourceful Huck brought the novel to life on the big screen

North - 1994 ($41 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Elijah Wood's portrayal of North, a young boy with an insatiable appetite for adventure, captured the hearts of audiences with his whimsical and endearing performance. With his wide-eyed wonder and natural charm, Wood helped make North a beloved cult favorite, grossing $41 million worldwide and showcasing Wood's ability to bring a touch of magic to the big screen.

The Good Son - 1993 ($60 Million)

Via Flickr/JustWatch

In this psychological thriller, Elijah Wood played a troubled child with a dark side, giving a performance that was so chilling and convincing, it made audiences question their own sanity. In fact, his performance was so convincing that some viewers reportedly had nightmares and trouble sleeping after seeing the film, solidifying Elijah Wood's reputation as a versatile actor capable of delivering intense and memorable performances. The Good Son grossed $60 million at the worldwide box office.

Flipper - 1996 ($86 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this family-friendly adventure film, Elijah Wood starred as Sandy Ricks, a rebellious teenager who spends the summer with his uncle in the Bahamas and forms a special bond with a dolphin named Flipper. With his signature charm and relatable portrayal of a troubled youth, Wood helped make Flipper a fan favorite. This charm helped it gross $86 million worldwide, making it a box office hit.

Sin City - 2005 ($158 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Elijah Wood's portrayal of Kevin, a silent, cannibalistic serial killer, was a bold departure from his previous roles and showcased his range as an actor. Wood's chilling and unforgettable performance brought a sense of terror to the gritty crime thriller and helped it gross $158 million worldwide, cementing Wood's status as a versatile and talented actor.

Deep Impact - 1998 ($349 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Elijah Wood played Leo Biederman, a teenage amateur astronomer who discovers a comet on a collision course with Earth in this disaster film. With his natural charisma and intelligence, Wood brought a relatable and human element to the role, earning critical acclaim and helping the film gross $349 million worldwide. Not only did Elijah Wood's character Leo Biederman showcase a remarkable understanding of astronomy, but his courage and determination in the face of an impending global catastrophe inspired audiences worldwide.

Happy Feet - 2006 ($384 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In Happy Feet, Elijah Wood brought Mumble to life with his exuberant voice-acting skills, infusing the character with contagious energy and endearing quirkiness. Wood's performance captured the hearts of audiences worldwide and helped make the film a modern animated classic, grossing an impressive $384 million at the box office. It's no wonder that his voice-acting skills have been sought after for many other animated films and TV shows.

The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers - 2002 ($947 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In the second chapter of Peter Jackson's beloved trilogy, Elijah Wood reprised his role as Frodo Baggins, delving deeper into the character's complex psyche and inner turmoil. Wood's nuanced and emotionally powerful performance perfectly captured the weight of Frodo's burden, cementing him as a fan favorite and helping the film gross a staggering $947 million worldwide.

The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey - 2012 ($1.02 Billion)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In the first installment of Peter Jackson's The Hobbit trilogy, Elijah Wood made a memorable cameo appearance as Frodo Baggins, bridging the gap between the beloved Lord of the Rings franchise and its prequel. Wood's poignant and heartfelt performance added an emotional resonance that fans couldn't resist, making his brief appearance all the more powerful. With Wood's star power and the film's epic adventure, The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey grossed an astonishing $1.02 billion worldwide.
