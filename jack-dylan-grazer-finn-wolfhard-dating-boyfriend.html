Why Fans Thought Finn Wolfhard Was Dating Jack Dylan Grazer

Publish date: 2024-08-07

In 2021, people all over the world were mesmerized and disgusted when Framing Britney Spears provided them with a depiction of what it is like for stars who are hounded by the press. On the bright side, a lot happened after the release of Framing Britney Spears and many of her fans now look at the media differently. Despite that, it seems clear that the press is unlikely to change its behavior since millions of people still avidly follow the tabloids.

For proof of the fact that a lot of people still see the personal lives of many stars as their entertainment, all you have to do is look at all the attention that people pay when celebrity couples break up. On top of that, a lot of people become convinced that certain stars are dating even if they aren’t. For example, even though it is abundantly clear that Jack Dylan Grazer and Finn Wolfhard have never been a couple, some people are convinced that they have been.

The Reason Why Some People Think That Jack Dylan Grazer And Finn Wolfhard Are A Couple

In the year 2017, horror movie fans everywhere lined up to see the movie It, a film that focused on a group of kids fighting against an evil force that took the lives of their peers. Fortunately for everyone involved in that film, it was a massive success at the box office and fully embraced by viewers. Of course, there were many reasons why the film was such a success including Stephen King’s engrossing story and Bill Skarsgård's amazing portrayal of Pennywise. In fact, the film’s main villain was so freaky and popular that Skarsgård continues to be asked about playing Pennywise again.

Related: Pennywise: 20 Secrets About The Clown’s Anatomy

On top of the fact that horror film fans love Bill Skarsgård’s performance in It, the group of kids who starred in the 2017 movie has been highly praised as well. The reason for that is that all of them did a wonderful job making audiences invest in them as a group of friends that have been thrust into a terrifying situation.

When 2017’s It was released, the film’s young cast took part in a series of interviews in which it became clear that they all got along extremely well. Even though all of the It kids seemed close, it didn’t take long for it to become clear that Jack Dylan Grazer And Finn Wolfhard had a special bond. In fact, some people have even gone so far as to edit together video clips that showed the two actors having fun together and expressing their mutual admiration.

Related: How Much Is ‘Stranger Things’ Star Finn Wolfhard Worth?

For most people who took note of Grazier and Wolfhard’s obvious friendship, it was cute to see. For other people, however, seeing Grazier and Wolfhard displaying their friendship was evidence that the two actors actually were a couple in real life. Given the fact that Wolfhard and Grazier were children at the time people came to that conclusion, it is weird that anyone felt the need to gossip about them like that.

Amazingly enough, anyone who wanted proof of the fact that Finn Wolfhard and Jack Dylan Grazer really were a couple was seemingly provided proof by Wikipedia at one point. After all, Grazier’s Wikipedia page briefly stated that the young actor was “dating Finn Wolfhard”.

Of course, it should go without saying that Wikipedia is an extremely useful resource. However, since anyone can edit a Wikipedia page, people should know to check sources whenever they read anything on the website and this story is a perfect example of why that is necessary. Thankfully, Grazer’s Wikipedia page was quickly edited to remove the reference to Wolfhard. However, loads of people would have read the actors were a couple by then and believed that was the case.

Jack Dylan Grazer Weighed In On The Rumors About His Relationship With Finn Wolfhard

If Jack Dylan Grazer and Finn Wolfhard developed feelings for one another and began dating once they were old enough, it would be great if they made each other happy. However, the truth of the matter is that all of the evidence points to the two young actors being friends and nothing more than that. Most notably, Grazer once denied that he and Wolfhard were a couple that believers have nicknamed Fack during a Livestream Q and A. While he didn’t seem bothered by the rumors, Grazer also asked fans to move on from the rumors.

“‘Fack’. Come on, guys, come on. Let’s give ‘Fack’ a rest, please. [...] It’s kind of annoying. [...] Guys, I don’t have a gay relationship with Finn, we all know that. It’s a joke.” What does it say about society that two male children can’t be open about their mutual admiration without people concluding they are a couple? On top of that, it is equally weird for similar reasons that so many people wanted Wolfhard and Millie Bobby Brown to become a couple in real life when they were kids.
