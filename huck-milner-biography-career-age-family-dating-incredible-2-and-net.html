Huck Milner Biography

 Huck Milner is an American voice actor. He made his feature film debut at the age of 10 when he appeared in the popular animated series Incredibles 2 in 2018. He lent his voice to the character named Dashiell “Dash” Parr by replacing Spencer Fox from the first film. Dash is Parr family’s troublemaker first son, who has superhuman speed.

He discovered his passion for acting when he followed his sisters into community theatre at 4 years old. His professional debut was the role of Dash in Incredibles 2, replacing Spencer Fox.

Milner continues to act professionally in theatre and voice-overs. Besides acting, he enjoys hockey, skiing, and playing the bass. He loves Thor, Ironman, and Spiderman. Milner has stated that he would like to one day eliminate homelessness in New York City.
