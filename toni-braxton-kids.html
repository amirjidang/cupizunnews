Who Are Toni Braxtons Kids? Where Are They Now?

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Toni Braxton is a highly celebrated American legend. With over 70 million records sold, nine Billboard Music Awards, seven American Music Awards, and seven Grammy Awards, Braxton has undeniably made her mark in the music and entertainment industry.

Born on October 7, 1967, she is the proud mother of two sons – Diezel and Denim Cole. Though they have grown up with celebrity status, they have both remained humble while simultaneously making their own paths in life due to the guidance and support from their beloved mother. 

Through success comes great responsibility, and Toni Braxton’s sons are no exception.

About Diezel Ky Braxton-Lewis?

Diezel comes from an illustrious and affluent family. His mother, Toni Lewis, is 55 years old, born on October 7th, 1967, while his father, Keri Lewis, is 52 years old; he was born on February 12th, 1971, and is the vocalist and songwriter for the renowned band Mint Condition that was founded in 1991.

In addition to this, his older brother Denim Cole Braxton Lewis is 21 years old, having been born on December 2nd, 2001. The parents of Diezel’s first encountered each other during a tour and afterward soon began their relationship. 

On April 21st, 2001, they tied the knot but unfortunately divorced after 8 short years in 2009.

In 2013, Diezel’s parents ended their marriage and began a new relationship. This new reality posed many challenges, yet they rose to the occasion and continued to provide an atmosphere of love and support for their children even while having separate lives.

They prioritize being present in their kids’ lives, showing up at recitals, games, award ceremonies and graduations.

Through it all, they have crafted an admirable co-parenting relationship that is based on respect for each other and encouragement for the prosperity of their children. 

Undoubtedly, this example has laid a strong foundation for Diezel’s success as a professional model.

Diezel’s presence on social media, especially Instagram, has been consistently strong and growing. With a large following of fans and admirers, Diezel often posts pictures from his photoshoots or projects to his accounts.

His collaborations with globally recognized brands such as Dior, Uncle Bud’s Hemp and CBD, Stars Fashion House and being represented by the prestigious Wilhelmina Modeling Agency have only furthered Diezel’s success in broadcasting his work on social media outlets.

About Denim Cole Braxton Lewis?

At 21 years of age, Denim Cole Braxton Lewis is the son of R&B star Toni Braxton and extremely successful entertainer Keri Lewis.

In the 90s, Toni topped the charts as one of the most acclaimed female artists in R&B history. His younger brother, Diezel Ky Braxton-Lewis, was born in March 2003 to the same formidable pair.

Michael Conrad Braxton and Evelyn Braxton are his proud grandparents. Denim currently cherishes a strong relationship with Makaila Teagle which commentators suggest began in 2018 when he commemorated their love on Instagram with an image captioned “HOCO” featuring himself and his partner. 

Since this point, Denim has posted regularly, including images documenting their relationship together, suggesting that their bond is ever-strengthening.
