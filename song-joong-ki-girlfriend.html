Who is Song Joong Ki’s girlfriend? This was trending on the internet when those having a crush on the Vincenzo actor heard that he was in a relationship.

Song was born on September 19, 1985, and he is regarded as a famous South Korean actor. He has been known for starring in Running Man (2010–2011), Sungkyunkwan Scandal (2010), Descendants of the Sun (2016), Arthdal Chronicles (2019), Vincenzo (2021), and Reborn Rich (2022).

In the hit K-drama, actor Song played the role of Vincenzo Cassano. Vincenzo is the Mafia consigliere and the Italian lawyer. Though he was of Korean descent, he was adopted by an Italian family when he was young. 

Song Joong Ki made it official in Singapore that he was in a relationship with someone. Let’s find out who Song Joong Ki’s girlfriend is.

Song Joong Ki girlfriend

According to Allkpop, at a media conference held on December 7 in Singapore, the staff members were introduced to Song’s girlfriend. 

It was speculated that his girlfriend was introduced at The Coliseum at Hard Rock Hotel at Resorts World Sentosa. It was known that he was there to promote Reborn Rich. 

He is a well-known actor and is popular for his role in the Netflix series Vincenzo. He is no stranger to the people of Singapore as he has visited there eight times. It was reported that all his trips were to the sunny island. 

On December 26, it was confirmed by his agency HighZium Studio that Song is dating someone, and it is speculated to be British.

Song was married to his wife, and it was reported that they met each other on the popular Korean drama Descendants Of The Sun in 2016. Two years after their marriage, they filed for divorce in 2019.
