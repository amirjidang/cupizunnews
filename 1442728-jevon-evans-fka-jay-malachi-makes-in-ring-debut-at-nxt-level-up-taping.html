JeVon Evans (fka Jay Malachi) Makes In-Ring Debut At NXT Level Up Taping

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Je’Von Evans has made his debut.

Prior to WWE NXT going live on February 6, matches for the February 9 episode of NXT Level Up were taped at the WWE Performance Center in Orlando. According to fans in attendance, Je’Von Evans, formerly known as Jay Malachi, made his in-ring debut against Brooks Jensen.

Malachi was reportedly among those offered WWE deals at the end of 2023 in addition to fellow independent stars Lucky Ali and Brogan Finlay. He was also a former Deadlock Pro Wrestling Worlds Champion until he vacated it in November.

WrestleZone will provide more information as details become available.
