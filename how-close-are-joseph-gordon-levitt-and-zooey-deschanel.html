How Close Are Joseph Gordon-Levitt And Zooey Deschanel?

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Celebrity couples and friendships get covered a ton by the media, and the fans get an inside peak at their interesting lives. Famous figures like Jennifer Aniston, Miley Cyrus, and Kim Kardashian are always in the headlines, and their friendships and relationships always make for the top stories. It may get old for some, but these stories sell.

Zooey Deschanel and Joseph Gordon-Levitt have been friends for years now, but most people have no idea how deep their friendship actually goes. Rare is it to see two talented people like this collaborate in more than one way, but these two have done some impressive stuff together.

Let's look and see just how close these two actually are!

They Made '(500) Days Of Summer' Together

Hollywood is a place where many talented individuals are able to get together to take part in fantastic projects, and this is precisely what happened when Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel linked up to make the film (500) Days of Summer.

This particular project wound up seeing the two stars display an exceptional amount of chemistry on the big screen, and people quickly fell in love with what they brought to the table in the film. In fact (500) Days of Summer has been considered to be one of the better romantic comedies of its era, which truly says a lot.

Bringing talented people together doesn’t always go as planned for film studios, but Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel were simply dynamite together and they wound up forming a close friendship in real life.

A decade after the film was released, the pair exchanged public messages on social media, according to People, and fans promptly lost their minds. Even though both performers have gone on to do bigger and better things, there is clearly still a massive amount of people who still thoroughly love everything that (500) Days of Summer brought to the table.

Most people know that Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel are great actors, especially when working together, but people were pretty surprised to see what happened when they tapped into another area of the entertainment industry.

They’ve Made Music Together

(500) Days of Summer was a successful project for Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel, but the pair have also worked together on musical endeavors.

It has been noted over time that Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel are both talented musicians, and while neither of them will ever top the Billboard charts, they still possess a ton of talent. So, when people learned that the pair had performed together, they immediately dropped everything that they were doing in order to hear the final product.

The clip of Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel performing music together on YouTube was something that took the Internet by storm. Even though the recording wasn’t of the highest quality, it was still really cool to see each of them out of their typical acting element and doing something that they clearly loved

Naturally, with a ton of chemistry in both film and music, people have been wondering whether or not Zooey Deschanel and Joseph Gordon-Levitt would ever consider dating one another.

They’re Friends Who Won’t Date

Hollywood is an interesting place, and the fans immediately jump to conclusions when they see two famous people spending a lot of time together. Because of their chemistry, fans have long wondered whether or not Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel would ever get into a relationship.

The truth of the matter is that the two of them have been friends for over a decade now, and even though the chemistry that they have is pretty fantastic to see, in reality, neither of them are particularly interested in taking things any further.

During an interview with Playboy, Joseph Gordon-Levitt would elaborate on the way that he feels about Zooey Deschanel and the potential of the two ever linking up romantically.

He would say, “It’s awkward when people say that. Whatever. Zooey and I just think it’s funny.”

As interesting as it would be for fans to see these two finally come together in a romantic way, sometimes, it’s better to just leave things as they are. Because they are such close friends, people are hoping that they will eventually work on another film project together.
