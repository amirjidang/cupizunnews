It has been six years since Doctor Strange has joined the Marvel Cinematic Universe through the film Dr. Strange in 2016. Although Dr. Strange has played main roles in numerous MCU releases since, it is only now that the second installment of the film is finally coming. The filming for the second installment of the film was halted due to the departure of Scott Derrickson as the director of the film and other MCU films as well.

A lot of the actors will be reprising their roles in the film such as Benedict Cumberbatch, Rachel McAdams, Benedict Wong, a lot more. However there will also be numerous addition to the cast and cameos this time like Elizabeth Olsen as Wanda Maximoff. Take a peek at some of the actors who will star in Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness and their net worth.

Xochitl Gomez

Xochitl Gomez plays as America Chavez in the film. America Chavez is a teenager who is originally from another universe which is known as Utopian Parallel. America has the ability to travel across the dimensions which she received when she punched open doorways as a kid. The American actress currently has a net worth of $1 million which she earned from various sources but mainly from acting. The actress is best known for her potrayal of the character Dawn Schafer for the Netflix series titled The Baby Sitters Club.

Benedict Wong

Benedict Wong plays as Wong in the film. Wong is one of the mentors of Dr. Strange who later on became his friend. Wong is now tasked to protect the most valuable relics and books at Kamar-Taj. He will also have numerous alternate versions of Wong including the Defender Wong. Among the fans, Wong’s character is one of the well loved characters based on the reviews of Dr. Strange. British-Hong Kong actor Benedict Wong has a net worth of $3 million. He has appeared on numerous films, TV series and stage plays. Other than his Dr. Strange character, he is also best known for his portrayal as Kublai Khan in Netflix’s Marco Polo and Bruce Ng in The Martian.

Hayley Atwell

Hayley Atwell plays Captain Carter/Peggy Carter in the film. She will appear as the version of Steve Roger's true love that has a superpower from another alternate universe. She has become a super-soldier and the SHIELD founder. English actress Hayley Atwell has a current net worth of $3 million. Other than her role as Peggy Carter in some of the Marvel Cinematic Universe films like in Captain America, she has played numerous roles including The Duchess, TV series Black Mirror, the series The Pillars of the Earth and a lot more. She is known to be great in fashion as people seemed to have a hard time taking their eyes off Atwell with her style.

Michael Stuhlbarg

Michael Stuhlbarg plays as Dr. Nicodemus West. Dr Nicodemus West is a surgeon who saved Dr. Strange’s life after his fatal car crash during the first installment of the film despite them being rivals. He was also the one who took charge in the operation to save The Ancient One although it was an unsuccessful operation. Michael Stuhlbarg has a current net worth of $3 million. He had numerous acting jobs including roles in the films such as Steve Jobs, Call Me By Your Name, Lincoln and a lot more.

Elizabeth Olsen

Elizabeth Olsen plays as Scarlett Witch / Wanda Maximoff. She is one of the Avengers who can harness magic through engaging telepathy and telekinesis and can even alter reality. In the film, she will help Strange with regards with the multiverse while the film will show a lot of various alternate versions of her character. American actress Elizabeth Olsen has a net worth of $11 million. Although years prior to her Hollywood career, she was only in the shadows of her famous sisters, Mary-Kate and Ashley. However through the years, she was able to build her Hollywood career which eventually led Elizabeth to become a Hollywood A-lister.

Chiwetel Ejiofor

Chiwetel Ejiofor plays as Karl Mordo, the former master of Mystic Arts. He started off in the first installment of the film as the mentor of Strange however he has become his nemesis as they started hunting the other sorcerers. Just like the other characters, Ejiofor plays the alternate versions of his character. According to Idol Net Worth, British actor Chiwetel Ejiofor has a net worth of $12 million thanks to his acting career. The actor has been nominated numerous times including an Academy Award nomination for the category of Best Actor.

Rachel McAdams

Rachel McAdams plays as Dr. Cristine Palmer. She plays as the former colleague and an ex lover of Dr. Strange. She is now about to get married after Dr. Strange decided to protect New York Sanctum instead of being with her in the previous film. Just like the other characters, she will also have different versions from the parallel universe in the film. Canadian actress Rachel McAdams has a current net worth of $25 million. She is best known for appearing in numerous hit films such as Mean Girls, The Notebook, Sherlock Holmes, About Time and a lot more. She is among the pretty faces in Hollywood and Rachel even looks great with bright red hair.

Benedict Cumberbatch

Benedict Cumberbatch reprises his role in the film as Dr. Strange. His character has had a massive character development as he used to be an arrogant neurosurgeon in the first film, however as the story evolves, his character becomes heroic. In the latest film, he will be playing an alternate version of himself which includes Sinister Strange who is someone corrupted version of his current self. British actor Benedict Cumberbatch has a current net worth of $40 million. He started his acting career since 2000 and he has never stopped working ever since. He is best known for his role as Sherlock in the BBC series Sherlock Holmes.
