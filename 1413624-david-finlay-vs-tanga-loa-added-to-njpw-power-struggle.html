David Finlay vs. Tanga Loa Added To NJPW Power Struggle

Publish date: 2024-08-28

NJPW continues to fill out the card for NJPW Power Struggle on November 4.

NJPW announced that former NEVER Openweight Champion David Finlay will face Tanga Loa at the show.

The preview, courtesy of NJPW’s website, is as follows:

“Meanwhile David Finlay will take on Tanga Loa in singles action. Less than one week after he lost the NEVER Openweight Championship to Tama Tonga in Ryogoku, Finlay was again reeling from a loss to Loa in tag competition in London. A frustrated and humiliated Finlay demanded this singles match to gain fast and full revenge, but could the Silverback beat the Rebel again?”

NJPW also confirmed that Will Ospreay will defend the IWGP United Kingdom (United States) Championship against Shota Umino, and Jon Moxley will face Great-O-Khan.

  • : Will Ospreay (c) vs. Shota Umino
  • : Hiromu Takahashi (c) vs. Taiji Ishimori
  • : Kazuchika Okada, Hiroshi Tanahashi & Tomohiro Ishii (c) vs. Zack Sabre Jr, Shane Haste & Mikey Nicholls
  • Jon Moxley vs. Great O-Khan
  • SANADA & Yuya Uemura vs. Tetsuya Naito & Yota Tsuji
  • Super Junior Tag League Finals
  • David Finlay vs. Tanga Loa

WrestleZone will have coverage of NJPW Power Struggle once it airs on November 4.
