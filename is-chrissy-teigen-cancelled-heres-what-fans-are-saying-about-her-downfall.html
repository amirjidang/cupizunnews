Is Chrissy Teigen Cancelled? Here's What Fans Are Saying About Her Downfall

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Chrissy Teigen is teeter tottering on the edge of being canceled for good, and it would be tough for her to blame anyone but herself. Chrissy's very own texts have risen to the surface, portraying her in a very negative light, and unless her phone was taken over by someone else, this sticky situation might prove to be impossible to rise above.

Many fans consider her to be canceled already and have turned against the star, while others believe that she still has an opportunity to redeem herself, if she takes her next steps very carefully. Here's what fans have to say about Chrissy Teigen today.

10 She's Creepy

Some of Chrissy Teigen's messages are downright creepy, and fans are calling her out on that very point. Now that her dirty laundry has been aired, many texts are rising to the surface that fans are deeming as being "creepy," and Teigen has yet to explain or make sense of them. Fans are now holding her accountable for some very questionable comments she has made.

Sadly, there has been a bubble burst where Chrissy Teigen's personality is concerned. Many of her fans know her best for her stunning swimwear photo shoots and her delectable cooking recipes. However, people are now seeing her true colors shine through, and fans are realizing that they just aren't as pretty as they had initially hoped.

8 Too Many Apologies, Not Enough Change

Chrissy Teigen has gone from being silent, to issuing apologies for her poor choices. Initially, fans were relieved that she stepped up to the plate and said sorry for what she had done wrong. Now, it seems that she has mastered the art of issuing public apologies, and fans are no longer fooled. There are too many apologies and not enough change.

7 She's Not Good Enough For John Legend

John Legend's reputation is a pretty wholesome one, and he has seen great success in his life. His fans are cautioning him to separate himself from Teigen, as she is proving to have a negative impact on his life. Being married to Teigen and supportive of her behavior is now starting to reflect badly on Legend, and fans are urging him to separate from her.

6 There's Nowhere To Hide

Fans are considering canceling Teigen due to the fact that so much has been exposed, and they're saddened by the fact that she was hiding behind the facade of her air brushed social media. 5 She's Worse Than Trump

Not only are fans accusing Teigen of acting badly, but they're even going so far as to say that she's "worse than Trump." The parallel being drawn is clear. Donald Trump was kicked off of social media for poor choices laced with hatred and the same could happen to her. 4 She's Sick To Wish Death On People

Teigen's situation is far worse than cyber bullying. The mere fact that she is encouraging people to take their own lives suggests that she is sick and willing to take things far past the normal levels of criticism. Fans are not willing to accept that she wishes death upon people, at all. Many are suggesting she has a serious mental problem that requires interference by professionals.

When Chrissy Teigen is under fire, she tends to recoil and become reclusive. Sadly, fans celebrate each time she falls off the grid, and many have commented that their social media posts are far less obtrusive when she is gone. When Chrissy takes a beak, fans feel this is a welcome change.

Some fans have questioned the relationship between John Legend and Chrissy Teigen and are wondering if she is bullying him on a daily basis.
